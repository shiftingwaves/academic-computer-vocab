## 📘 Word: **Correspondence**

---

## 1. 🧠 Basic Meaning

**Correspondence** means communication between two people or groups by letters, emails, or messages.
(Do logon ya groups ke darmiyan likh kar ki jany wali baat cheet)

It also means things that match or are connected with each other.
(Do cheezon ka aapas mein milna ya relate karna)

---

## 2. 🇵🇰 Roman Urdu Meaning

**Correspondence = khat o kitaabat / rabta / milta julta hona**
(Logon ke darmiyan likh kar rabta karna ya cheezon ka match karna)

---

## 3. ⭐ Why This Word Matters

This word is important because communication is a big part of study, work, and computer systems.
(Ye word important hai kyun ke communication har field mein use hota hai)

---

## 4. 🌍 Daily-Life Usage

We use correspondence when we send emails or letters to someone.
(Hum jab email ya khat bhejte hain to ye word use hota hai)

It also means when two things are similar or match.
(Jab do cheezen milti julti hon)

---

## 5. 🎓 Academic Usage

Students use correspondence in formal writing and communication with teachers or universities.
(Students official emails ya letters mein use karte hain)

It is also used in research communication.
(Research mein bhi formal rabta hota hai)

---

## 6. 💻 Computer Science Usage

In computer science, correspondence means matching data between systems or programs.
(CS mein data ka match hona systems ke darmiyan)

It is used in messaging systems and email protocols.
(Messaging aur email systems mein use hota hai)

It can also describe mapping between inputs and outputs.
(Input aur output ka relation)

---

## 7. 📖 Beginner Explanation

Think of correspondence as “sending messages” or “things that match.”
(Isay simple samjho ke message bhejna ya cheezon ka match hona)

---

## 8. 🔁 Synonyms vs Similar Words

**Synonyms:**

* communication (rabta)
* exchange (tabadla)
* messaging (paigham bhejna)

**Similar words:**

* connection (rabta)
* relation (taaluq)

---

## 9. 🚫 Antonyms

* silence (khamoshi)
* misunderstanding (ghalat fehmi)
* disconnect (rabta khatam hona)

---

## 10. 🌱 Word Family

* correspond (verb)
* correspondent (person who reports news)
* correspondence (noun)
* corresponding (adjective)

---

## 11. 💬 Common Phrases

* email correspondence (email rabta)
* business correspondence (karobari khat o kitaabat)
* correspondence course (distance learning course)

---

## 12. 🧠 Memory Technique

Think: **“correspond = connect + respond”**
(Jab respond karte hain to message ka jawab milta hai → correspondence)

---

## 13. ⚠️ Common Mistakes Students Make

* Thinking it only means “letters” (sirf khat samajhna)
* Confusing it with “correction” (galti se milana)
* Using it in informal chatting incorrectly (casual chat mein formal word use karna)

---

# ✍️ Sentences

---

## 1. 🏠 3 Easy Daily-Life Sentences

* I had correspondence with my friend through email. (Main ne email ke zariye dost se rabta kiya)
* Their correspondence lasted for many years. (Unka rabta kai saalon tak chalta raha)
* There is a correspondence between these two letters. (In dono letters mein milta julta content hai)

---

## 2. 🎓 3 Academic Sentences

* The student maintained correspondence with the professor. (Student ne professor se formal rabta rakha)
* Academic correspondence must be clear and formal. (Academic rabta saaf aur formal hona chahiye)
* The university checked all correspondence carefully. (University ne saari communication check ki)

---

## 3. 💻 5 Computer Science Sentences (ACTIVE VOICE)

* The system sends correspondence between two servers. (System do servers ke darmiyan message bhejta hai)
* The program maps correspondence between input and output. (Program input aur output ka relation banata hai)
* Developers use correspondence to connect user data. (Developers user data ko connect karte hain)
* The application handles correspondence in real time. (Application real time mein communication handle karta hai)
* Engineers design correspondence protocols for networks. (Engineers networks ke liye communication rules design karte hain)

---

## 4. 💻 5 Computer Science Sentences (PASSIVE VOICE)

* Correspondence is sent between servers by the system. (System ke zariye servers ke darmiyan message bheja jata hai)
* The mapping of input and output is defined as correspondence. (Input aur output ka relation correspondence ke taur par define hota hai)
* User data is connected through correspondence by developers. (User data developers ke zariye connect kiya jata hai)
* Communication is handled in real time through correspondence. (Communication real time mein correspondence ke zariye handle hota hai)
* Network protocols are designed for correspondence between devices. (Network protocols devices ke darmiyan rabta ke liye banaye jate hain)
