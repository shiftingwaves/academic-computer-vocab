# **Permutation**

## 1. Basic Meaning

A **permutation** means arranging things in a **specific order**.
(Ya cheezon ko aik khaas tarteeb mein rakhna hota hai)

Example:
A, B, C → ABC and CBA are different permutations.
(Yahan har alag order ko alag arrangement maana jata hai)

---

## 2. Roman Urdu Meaning

**Permutation = Tarteeb badal kar arrangement banana**
(Yani cheezon ko mukhtalif order mein lagana)

---

## 7. Beginner Explanation

If the **order matters**, it is usually a **permutation**.
(Agar order important ho to usay permutation kehte hain)

Example:

* 123 and 321 are different permutations.
  (Dono numbers ka order alag hai)

---

## 13. Common Mistakes Students Make

### ❌ Mistake 1:

Thinking permutation and combination are the same.
(Kai students dono ko same samajhte hain)

### ✔ Correct:

Permutation → order matters
Combination → order may not matter
(Yahan order hi main difference hota hai)

---

### ❌ Mistake 2:

Ignoring order in programming problems.
(Programming questions mein order ko ignore kar dete hain)
