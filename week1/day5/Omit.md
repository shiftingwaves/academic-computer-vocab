## 📘 Word: **Omit**

---

## 1. 🎯 Basic Meaning

**Omit** means to **leave something out** or **not include something**. (Omit ka matlab kisi cheez ko jaan boojh kar ya galti se shamil na karna hota hai)

---

## 2. 🗣️ Roman Urdu Meaning

**Omit = chhor dena / include na karna** (matlab kisi cheez ko list, sentence ya system mein na dalna)

---

## 3. ⭐ Why This Word Matters

In studies and programming, we often **omit unnecessary data or steps**. (Hum zindagi aur programming mein extra cheezen chhor dete hain jo zaroori nahi hoti)

---

## 4. 🌍 Daily-Life Usage

We use "omit" when we forget or skip something in writing or speaking. (Hum jab koi cheez likhne ya bolne mein chhor dete hain to omit use hota hai)

---

## 5. 🎓 Academic Usage

In exams or writing, students may **omit details by mistake**. (Students exams ya writing mein kuch details chhor dete hain)

---

## 6. 💻 Computer Science Usage

In programming, we can **omit optional parameters in functions**. (Programming mein hum optional cheezen function mein chhor sakte hain)

---

## 7. 🧠 Beginner Explanation

Think of “omit” as **not adding something that could be there**. (Isko aise samjho ke koi cheez add honi thi lekin tumne nahi ki)

---

## 8. 🔁 Synonyms vs Similar Words

* **Skip** (chhor dena)
* **Leave out** (bahar chhor dena)
* **Exclude** (shamil na karna)

---

## 9. ❌ Antonyms

* Include (shamil karna)
* Add (jodna)
* Mention (zikr karna)

---

## 10. 📚 Word Family

* Omit (verb)
* Omission (noun)
* Omitted (past form)
* Omitting (continuous form)

---

## 11. 💬 Common Phrases

* Omit details (details chhor dena)
* Omit from list (list se chhor dena)
* Accidentally omit (galti se chhor dena)

---

## 12. 🧠 Memory Technique

Think: **Omit = “Oh! missed it”** (Oh! main ne chhor diya) (yaad rakhne ke liye socho kuch reh gaya)

---

## 13. ⚠️ Common Mistakes Students Make

* Students confuse **omit with delete** (omit aur delete ko same samajh lete hain)
* They think it always means mistake (yeh zaroori nahi, kabhi jaan boojh kar bhi hota hai)
* They forget it can be used in programming too (CS mein bhi use hota hai)

---

# ✍️ SENTENCES

---

## 1. 🌍 3 Easy Daily-Life Sentences

* I omit my homework mistake in my notebook. (Main apni notebook mein homework ki galti chhor deta hoon)
* She omits sugar in tea sometimes. (Woh kabhi kabhi chai mein cheeni nahi dalti)
* We omit one step in the recipe. (Hum recipe ka ek step chhor dete hain)

---

## 2. 🎓 3 Academic Sentences

* The student omits important details in the answer. (Student jawab mein important details chhor deta hai)
* Teachers ask students not to omit any step in the solution. (Teachers kehte hain koi step chhorna nahi)
* He omits references in his assignment. (Woh apni assignment mein references chhor deta hai)

---

## 3. 💻 5 Computer Science Sentences (ACTIVE VOICE)

* The programmer omits optional parameters in the function. (Programmer function mein optional parameters chhor deta hai)
* The developer omits error handling in simple code. (Developer simple code mein error handling chhor deta hai)
* The system omits unnecessary logs during execution. (System execution ke dauran extra logs chhor deta hai)
* The coder omits duplicate data in the database. (Coder database mein duplicate data chhor deta hai)
* The engineer omits unused variables in the program. (Engineer program mein unused variables chhor deta hai)

---

## 4. 💻 5 Computer Science Sentences (PASSIVE VOICE)

* Optional parameters are omitted by the programmer in the function. (Optional parameters programmer ke zariye chhor diye jate hain)
* Error handling is omitted in simple code by the developer. (Simple code mein error handling developer ke zariye chhor di jati hai)
* Unnecessary logs are omitted by the system during execution. (System execution mein extra logs chhor deta hai)
* Duplicate data is omitted from the database by the coder. (Database se duplicate data chhor diya jata hai)
* Unused variables are omitted from the program by the engineer. (Program se unused variables chhor diye jate hain)
