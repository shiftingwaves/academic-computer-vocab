# **Vocabulary Word: “Diversity”**

## 1. Basic Meaning

**Diversity** means having many different kinds of people, ideas, things, or systems together.
(Mukhtalif qisam ke log ya cheezen aik jagah par hona)

---

## 2. Roman Urdu Meaning

**Diversity = Rang rang ki variety / mukhtalif cheezon ka majmooa**
(Yani sab log ya cheezen aik jaisi na hon)

---

## 3. Why This Word Matters

* It is used in schools, universities, and offices.
* It is important in teamwork and technology.
* In Computer Science, different ideas help make better software.
  (Mukhtalif soch behtar kaam karne mein madad deti hai)

---

## 4. Daily-Life Usage

We use **diversity** when talking about:

* Different cultures
* Different foods
* Different languages
* Different opinions
  (Rozmarra zindagi mein farq aur variety dikhane ke liye)

---

## 5. Academic Usage

In academics, **diversity** means:

* Different students
* Different research ideas
* Different learning methods
  (Taleem mein mukhtalif backgrounds aur ideas ko samjha jata hai)

---

## 6. Computer Science Usage

In Computer Science, diversity can mean:

* Different programming languages
* Different datasets
* Different users of software
* Different problem-solving methods
  (CS mein mukhtalif technologies aur users ki ahmiyat hoti hai)

---

## 7. Beginner Explanation

If one class has students from many cities, languages, and cultures, that class has **diversity**.
(Agar class mein har tarah ke students hon to usay diversity kehte hain)

---

## 8. Synonyms vs Similar Words

| Word           | Simple Meaning           |
| -------------- | ------------------------ |
| **Variety**    | Many different kinds     |
| **Difference** | Not the same             |
| **Mixture**    | Things combined together |
| **Inclusion**  | Everyone is welcomed     |

---

## 9. Antonyms

* **Sameness**
* **Uniformity**
* **Similarity**
  (Yani sab cheezen aik jaisi hona)

---

## 10. Word Family

| Word                | Type      | Meaning                   |
| ------------------- | --------- | ------------------------- |
| **Diversity**       | Noun      | Different kinds together  |
| **Diverse**         | Adjective | Different                 |
| **Diversify**       | Verb      | To make different         |
| **Diversification** | Noun      | Process of adding variety |

---

## 11. Common Phrases

* **Cultural diversity**
* **Diversity in technology**
* **Workplace diversity**
* **Diversity of ideas**
* **Biological diversity**

---

## 12. Memory Technique

Think of a **fruit basket** with bananas, apples, oranges, and mangoes.
Different fruits together = **diversity**.
(Mukhtalif phal aik tokri mein hon to diversity yaad rakho)

---

## 13. Common Mistakes Students Make

### ❌ Wrong:

“Diversity means only different people.”

### ✔ Correct:

Diversity can mean different people, ideas, systems, languages, or technologies.
(Diversity sirf logon ke liye nahi hoti)

# 🌍 3 Easy Daily-Life Sentences

1. Our neighborhood has cultural diversity.
   (Hamare ilaqe mein mukhtalif cultures ke log rehte hain)

2. The restaurant menu shows great diversity.
   (Menu mein har tarah ka khana mojood hai)

3. I enjoy diversity in music styles.
   (Mujhe alag alag music styles pasand hain)

---

# 📚 3 Academic Sentences

1. The university supports diversity among students.
   (University har background ke students ko support karti hai)

2. Diversity improves classroom discussions.
   (Mukhtalif soch class discussion ko behtar banati hai)

3. The research team values diversity of ideas.
   (Research team alag alag ideas ko ahmiyat deti hai)

---

# 💻 5 Computer Science Sentences (ACTIVE VOICE)

1. The developer uses diversity in training data.
   (Developer data mein variety rakhta hai)

2. Our team studies diversity in user behavior.
   (Hamari team users ke mukhtalif habits ko study karti hai)

3. The company hires programmers from diverse backgrounds.
   (Company har background ke programmers rakhti hai)

4. The engineer adds diversity to the dataset.
   (Engineer dataset ko zyada varied banata hai)

5. The software team encourages diversity in problem-solving.
   (Software team mukhtalif tareeqon se maslay hal karne ko promote karti hai)

---

# 💻 5 Computer Science Sentences (PASSIVE VOICE)

1. Diversity is included in the machine learning dataset.
   (Machine learning dataset mein variety shamil ki jati hai)

2. Different coding styles are accepted in the project for diversity.
   (Project mein mukhtalif coding styles ko accept kiya jata hai)

3. Diverse user data is collected by the application.
   (Application users ka mukhtalif data collect karti hai)

4. Diversity is encouraged during software development.
   (Software development mein alag soch ko promote kiya jata hai)

5. Multiple programming languages are used to increase diversity.
   (Variety barhane ke liye alag programming languages use ki jati hain)
