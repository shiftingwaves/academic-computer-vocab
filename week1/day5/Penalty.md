## 📘 Word: **Penalty**

---

## 1. ⭐ Basic Meaning

A **penalty** is a punishment or negative result when someone breaks a rule or makes a mistake.
(Yaani jab koi rule break kare to usay nateeja ya saza milti hai)

---

## 2. 🇵🇰 Roman Urdu Meaning

**Penalty = saza / nuksaan / negative result**
(Jab kisi ghalti ya rule break par nuksaan ya saza milay)

---

## 3. 🎯 Why This Word Matters

This word is important because rules exist everywhere—sports, exams, and computers. If rules are broken, a **penalty** happens.
(Is liye zaroori hai ke rules follow karein warna nuksaan hota hai)

---

## 4. 🌍 Daily-Life Usage

We see penalties in traffic, sports, and school rules.
(Rozmarra zindagi mein rules break karne par penalty milti hai)

---

## 5. 🎓 Academic Usage

In exams or school systems, penalties are given for cheating or late submission.
(Academics mein late kaam ya cheating par saza milti hai)

---

## 6. 💻 Computer Science Usage

In programming or systems, a **penalty** can mean reduced score, delay, or performance loss when rules or constraints are violated.
(Computer systems mein rule break hone par system performance ya score reduce ho jata hai)

---

## 7. 🧠 Beginner Explanation

Think of **penalty** as a “negative result” when you do something wrong.
(Jab kuch ghalat karo to jo bura nateeja milta hai woh penalty hai)

---

## 8. 🔁 Synonyms vs Similar Words

**Synonyms:**

* Punishment
* Fine
* Consequence
* Charge

**Similar Words:**

* Cost (nuksaan)
* Damage (nuqsan)

---

## 9. ❌ Antonyms

* Reward (inaam)
* Bonus
* Benefit
* Prize

---

## 10. 🧩 Word Family

* Penal (adjective)
* Penalize (verb)
* Penalty (noun)

---

## 11. 💬 Common Phrases

* penalty fee
* penalty shot
* time penalty
* penalty rule

---

## 12. 🧠 Memory Technique

Think: **“Penalty = Problem after mistake”**
(Jab mistake hoti hai to problem ya saza milti hai)

---

## 13. ⚠️ Common Mistakes Students Make

* Confusing **penalty** with “pen” (they are not related)
* Thinking it only belongs to sports
* Using it as a verb instead of noun
* Forgetting it means **negative result, not just punishment**

# ✍️ SENTENCES

---

## 1. 🌟 Daily-Life Sentences

* I got a penalty for breaking the traffic signal. (Mujhe traffic signal todne par saza mili)
* There is a penalty for late fee payment. (Late fee dene par nuksaan ya extra charge lagta hai)
* He faced a penalty for missing the school rules. (Usay school rule todne par saza mili)

---

## 2. 🎓 Academic Sentences

* Students get a penalty for submitting assignments late. (Students ko late assignment par saza milti hai)
* The school applies a penalty for cheating in exams. (School cheating par saza lagata hai)
* A penalty is given when rules are not followed in class. (Class rules na manne par saza milti hai)

---

## 3. 💻 Computer Science Sentences (ACTIVE VOICE)

* The system applies a penalty for incorrect input. (System ghalat input par nuksaan lagata hai)
* The algorithm calculates a penalty for slow performance. (Algorithm slow performance ka nuksaan nikalta hai)
* The program adds a penalty when memory usage increases. (Program memory zyada use hone par penalty lagata hai)
* Developers assign a penalty for rule violation in the code. (Developers code rule break par saza dete hain)
* The network causes a penalty for high latency. (Network high delay par nuksaan deta hai)

---

## 4. 💻 Computer Science Sentences (PASSIVE VOICE)

* A penalty is applied by the system for invalid data. (System ghalat data par penalty lagata hai)
* A penalty is calculated by the algorithm for errors. (Algorithm errors par nuksaan nikalta hai)
* A penalty is added by the program for memory overflow. (Program memory overflow par penalty lagata hai)
* A penalty is assigned by developers for rule breaking. (Developers rule todne par saza dete hain)
* A penalty is caused by the network due to delay. (Network delay ki wajah se nuksaan hota hai)
