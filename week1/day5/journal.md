# 📘 Word: **journal**

## 1. Basic Meaning

A **journal** is a notebook or digital record where someone writes information regularly.
(Roz likhne ya record rakhne wali copy ya file)

---

## 2. Roman Urdu Meaning

* **Roznamcha**
* **Record book**
* **Notes likhne ki jagah**

---

## 3. Why This Word Matters

The word **journal** is important because students, researchers, and programmers use journals to save ideas, notes, and records.
(Yeh lafz important hai kyun ke students aur programmers notes aur records save karte hain)

---

## 4. Daily-Life Usage

People write feelings, plans, or daily activities in a journal.
(Log apni roz ki baatein aur plans journal mein likhte hain)

---

## 5. Academic Usage

Students and teachers use journals for research and study notes.
(Students aur teachers research aur parhai ke notes journal mein rakhte hain)

---

## 6. Computer Science Usage

In Computer Science, a **journal** can mean a system record that stores changes or events.
(Computer Science mein journal system ke records aur changes save karta hai)

---

## 7. Beginner Explanation

Think of a **journal** like a diary or a logbook. It keeps important information safe.
(Journal diary jaisi cheez hoti hai jahan important baatein save hoti hain)

---

## 8. Synonyms vs Similar Words

| Word         | Meaning                     |
| ------------ | --------------------------- |
| **Diary**    | Personal daily writing      |
| **Notebook** | General writing book        |
| **Log**      | Record of events or actions |
| **Record**   | Saved information           |

---

## 9. Antonyms

* Forgetfulness
* Loss of records
* Unwritten notes

---

## 10. Word Family

| Word           | Type                   |
| -------------- | ---------------------- |
| **Journal**    | Noun                   |
| **Journalist** | Person who writes news |
| **Journalism** | News writing field     |
| **Journaling** | Writing regularly      |

---

## 11. Common Phrases

* **keep a journal**
* **research journal**
* **daily journal**
* **system journal**
* **journal entry**

---

## 12. Memory Technique

Think: **Journal = Journey of your thoughts**.
You write your daily journey in it.
(Journal ko apni sochon ki journey samjho)

---

## 13. Common Mistakes Students Make

❌ Saying “journal” only means a diary
✔ It can also mean a research paper collection or system record

❌ Confusing **journal** with **newspaper**
✔ A journal is usually for records, research, or personal writing

---

# ✍️ 1. Easy Daily-Life Sentences

* I write my plans in a journal. (Main apne plans journal mein likhta hoon)

* My sister keeps a daily journal. (Meri behan roz journal likhti hai)

* The journal is on the table. (Journal mez par rakha hai)

---

# 📚 2. Academic Sentences

* The student read a science journal. (Student ne science ka journal padha)

* Our teacher asked for a journal entry. (Teacher ne journal entry mangi)

* She writes research notes in her journal. (Woh apne research notes journal mein likhti hai)

---

# 💻 3. Computer Science Sentences (ACTIVE VOICE)

* The database stores errors in a journal. (Database errors ko journal mein save karta hai)

* The programmer checks the system journal daily. (Programmer roz system journal check karta hai)

* The application writes user activity to the journal. (Application user activity journal mein likhti hai)

* The server records login attempts in a journal. (Server login attempts journal mein record karta hai)

* The operating system saves updates in a journal. (Operating system updates journal mein save karta hai)

---

# 💻 4. Computer Science Sentences (PASSIVE VOICE)

* User actions are recorded in the journal. (User ki activities journal mein record ki jati hain)

* The backup data is stored in a journal file. (Backup data journal file mein save hota hai)

* System errors are written to the journal automatically. (System errors automatically journal mein likhe jate hain)

* Important events are saved in the system journal. (Important events system journal mein save kiye jate hain)

* The transaction history is maintained in a journal. (Transaction history journal mein maintain ki jati hai)
