## 📘 Word: **formally**

---

## 1. 🧠 Basic Meaning

“Formally” means doing something in a **proper, official, or correct way** according to rules.
(kaam ko qanooni ya proper tareeqay se karna)

---

## 2. 🇵🇰 Roman Urdu Meaning

**Formally = rasmi tor par / qanooni ya proper tareeqay se**
(jab koi cheez rules ya official style ke mutabiq ki jaye)

---

## 3. ⭐ Why This Word Matters

We use “formally” when we talk, write, or behave in a **professional or official situation**.
(yeh word professional aur official halat mein use hota hai)

---

## 4. 🌍 Daily-Life Usage

* In meetings, interviews, or letters, people speak formally.
  (meeting ya interview mein log proper tareeqay se baat karte hain)

---

## 5. 🎓 Academic Usage

* Teachers expect students to write answers formally in exams.
  (exam mein students se proper writing expect ki jati hai)

---

## 6. 💻 Computer Science Usage

* We formally define algorithms in textbooks.
  (textbooks mein algorithms ko proper definition ke sath likha jata hai)

* In programming, we formally describe system requirements.
  (programming mein system ki requirements ko proper tareeqay se explain karte hain)

* Scientists formally write documentation for software systems.
  (scientists software ki proper documentation likhte hain)

---

## 7. 👶 Beginner Explanation

“Formally” means you are not being casual or informal.
You are acting like in school exams, office work, or official emails.
(jese school ya office mein serious aur proper tareeqe se kaam karna)

---

## 8. 🔁 Synonyms vs Similar Words

* **Formally = officially, properly, professionally**
* Similar but different:

  * Informally (casual style)
  * Normally (usual way)

---

## 9. ⛔ Antonyms

* Informally
* Casually
* Unofficially
* Relaxed way

---

## 10. 📚 Word Family

* Formal (adjective)
* Formally (adverb)
* Formality (noun)
* Informal (opposite adjective)

---

## 11. 💬 Common Phrases

* formally defined
* formally written
* formally approved
* formally announced
* formally invited

---

## 12. 🧠 Memory Technique

Think:
**“Formal = office dress + serious rules”**
So “formally” means doing things in an **office-like serious way**.
(yaad rakho: formal matlab serious office style)

---

## 13. ⚠️ Common Mistakes Students Make

* ❌ Using “formally” in casual chatting
* ❌ Confusing it with “normally”
* ❌ Writing informal English in exams when formal is needed
* ❌ Thinking it only means “polite” (it also means official + proper)

# ✍️ SENTENCES

---

## 1. 🌍 Daily-Life Sentences

* I formally greeted my teacher at the school event. (Main ne school event mein apne teacher ko proper tareeqe se greet kiya)
* She formally accepted the invitation to the ceremony. (Us ne ceremony ka invite rasmi tor par accept kiya)
* They formally apologized for the mistake. (Unhon ne ghalti ke liye proper tareeqe se maafi maangi)

---

## 2. 🎓 Academic Sentences

* Students must formally write their exam answers. (Students ko apne exam answers proper style mein likhne chahiye)
* The university formally publishes exam results every semester. (University har semester results rasmi tor par announce karti hai)
* The teacher formally explained the new syllabus. (Teacher ne naya syllabus proper tareeqe se explain kiya)

---

## 3. 💻 Computer Science Sentences (ACTIVE VOICE)

* Developers formally define the algorithm before coding. (Developers coding se pehle algorithm ko proper tareeqe se define karte hain)
* The engineer formally documents the software design. (Engineer software design ki proper documentation banata hai)
* Programmers formally test the system requirements. (Programmers system requirements ko proper tareeqe se test karte hain)
* Scientists formally describe data structures in textbooks. (Scientists textbooks mein data structures ko properly explain karte hain)
* Teams formally review code before release. (Teams release se pehle code ko proper tareeqe se check karte hain)
