## 📘 Word: **Equivalence**

---

## 1. 🟢 Basic Meaning

**Equivalence** means *two things are equal in value, meaning, or effect*.

(yaani do cheezen asal mein barabar hon ya same result deti hon)

---

## 2. 🟢 Roman Urdu Meaning

**Equivalence = barabari / yaksaniyat / same hona (asli meaning mein)**
(yaani do cheezon ka result ya value ek jaisa ho)

---

## 3. 🟢 Why This Word Matters

This word is important because we compare things in math, logic, and computer science.
(kyunke hum har field mein cheezon ka comparison karte hain)

---

## 4. 🟢 Daily-Life Usage

We use equivalence when we say two things are the same in value or meaning.
(jab hum kehte hain do cheezen same hain ya barabar hain)

---

## 5. 🟢 Academic Usage

In academics, equivalence is used in math, logic, and equations.
(academic level par yeh math aur logic mein bohat use hota hai)

---

## 6. 🟢 Computer Science Usage

In CS, equivalence is used in logic gates, algorithms, and programming conditions.
(computer science mein yeh check karne ke liye hota hai ke do conditions same result deti hain ya nahi)

---

## 7. 🟢 Beginner Explanation

Think of equivalence as:
👉 “A = B” means both give the same result or meaning.
(yaani A aur B ka output ya value same ho)

---

## 8. 🟢 Synonyms vs Similar Words

**Synonyms:**

* equality
* sameness
* parity

**Similar Words (not exact):**

* similarity (sirf milti julti hoti hai, fully same nahi hoti)
* identity (exact same cheez)

---

## 9. 🟢 Antonyms

* inequality
* difference
* mismatch
* inequality condition

---

## 10. 🟢 Word Family

* equivalent (adjective)
* equivalently (adverb)
* equivalence (noun)

---

## 11. 🟢 Common Phrases

* logical equivalence
* equivalence relation
* equivalent expression
* equivalence class

---

## 12. 🟢 Memory Technique

Think:
👉 “Equal + Value = Equivalence”
(yaani jab value same ho, to equivalence hota hai)

---

## 13. 🟢 Common Mistakes Students Make

* Confusing **equivalence** with **similarity**
  (similar matlab same nahi hota)
* Thinking it means “exact copy”
* Using it without checking actual value or output

# ✍️ SENTENCES

---

## 1. 🟢 Daily-Life Sentences

* This price is equivalent to that one. (Yeh price us ke barabar hai)
* Two cups of tea are equivalent in quantity. (Dono chai ke cups quantity mein same hain)
* His effort is equivalent to hard work. (Uski mehnat asal mein bohat hard work ke barabar hai)

---

## 2. 🟢 Academic Sentences

* The equivalence of two expressions can be proven in algebra. (Algebra mein expressions ki barabari prove ki ja sakti hai)
* We study equivalence in mathematical logic. (Hum logic mein equivalence parhते hain)
* Equivalence helps compare different scientific models. (Equivalence se different models ka comparison hota hai)

---

## 3. 🟢 Computer Science Sentences (ACTIVE VOICE)

* The programmer checks equivalence between two conditions. (Programmer do conditions ki barabari check karta hai)
* The system compares equivalence of two algorithms. (System do algorithms ki equivalence compare karta hai)
* Developers use equivalence to simplify logic expressions. (Developers logic expressions ko simple karne ke liye equivalence use karte hain)
* The compiler evaluates equivalence in boolean expressions. (Compiler boolean expressions mein equivalence evaluate karta hai)
* Engineers test equivalence in digital circuits. (Engineers digital circuits mein equivalence test karte hain)

---

## 4. 🟢 Computer Science Sentences (PASSIVE VOICE)

* Equivalence is checked between two conditions by the program. (Do conditions ki barabari program ke zariye check hoti hai)
* Logical equivalence is used in compiler optimization. (Compiler optimization mein logical equivalence use hoti hai)
* Boolean expressions are evaluated for equivalence by the system. (System boolean expressions ki equivalence evaluate karta hai)
* Algorithms are compared for equivalence in analysis. (Algorithms analysis mein equivalence ke liye compare hote hain)
* Digital circuits are tested for equivalence during design. (Design ke waqt circuits ki equivalence test hoti hai)
