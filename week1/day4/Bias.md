## 📘 Word: **Bias**

---

## 1. 🧠 Basic Meaning

**Bias** means a **strong preference for one side**, even if it is not fair or balanced.
(bias ka matlab kisi ek taraf zyada jhukao ya pasand hona, jo hamesha fair na ho)

---

## 2. 🗣️ Roman Urdu Meaning

**Bias = kisi ek taraf na-insafi wala jhukao ya pasand**
(yaani jab aap bina proper reason ke kisi side ko zyada support karo)

---

## 3. ⭐ Why This Word Matters

Bias is important because it can affect:

* decisions
* learning
* technology results
* fairness

(kyun ke bias se faislay galat ho sakte hain aur fairness khatam ho sakti hai)

---

## 4. 🌍 Daily-Life Usage

* People can have bias in opinions, sports, or friends.
  (log aksar bina sochay kisi team ya dost ko prefer karte hain)

---

## 5. 🎓 Academic Usage

* Bias can affect research results.
  (research mein bias se results unfair ho sakte hain)

* Students should avoid bias in analysis.
  (students ko analysis mein neutral rehna chahiye)

---

## 6. 💻 Computer Science Usage

* AI models can show bias in predictions.
  (AI systems apne results mein jhukao dikha sakte hain)

* Training data bias can affect machine learning accuracy.
  (data ka bias model ki performance ko bigaar sakta hai)

---

## 7. 📖 Beginner Explanation

Bias is when something is **not neutral** and favors one side.
(yaani jab koi system ya insaan balanced na ho aur ek side ko zyada importance de)

---

## 8. 🔁 Synonyms vs Similar Words

**Synonyms:**

* Prejudice
* Preference
* Inclination

**Similar words:**

* Opinion
* Belief
* Judgment

---

## 9. ⛔ Antonyms

* Fairness
* Neutrality
* Impartiality
* Objectivity

---

## 10. 🔗 Word Family

* Biased (adjective)
* Biasing (verb form usage)
* Unbiased (no bias / fair)

---

## 11. 💬 Common Phrases

* Gender bias
* Data bias
* Unconscious bias
* Cultural bias
* Algorithmic bias

---

## 12. 🧠 Memory Technique

Think:
👉 “Bias = *Bye fairness*” 😄
(jab bias hota hai to fairness bye ho jati hai)

---

## 13. ⚠️ Common Mistakes Students Make

* Thinking bias is always intentional (yeh zaroori nahi, kabhi unconscious bhi hota hai)
* Confusing bias with opinion
* Not understanding bias in AI/data
* Using bias only for humans, not machines

---

# ✍️ Sentences

---

## 1. 🏠 3 Easy Daily-Life Sentences

* I should not show bias when choosing friends. (Main doston ko choose karte waqt na-insafi wala jhukao nahi rakhna chahiye)
* The teacher tried to avoid bias in class. (Teacher ne class mein fair rehne ki koshish ki)
* He has a bias toward his favorite team. (Usko apni pasandida team ki taraf zyada jhukao hai)

---

## 2. 🎓 3 Academic Sentences

* Bias can affect the quality of research. (Bias research ki quality ko affect kar sakta hai)
* Students must reduce bias in their conclusions. (Students ko apne nateejon mein bias kam karna chahiye)
* Scientific studies should avoid personal bias. (Scientific studies ko personal jhukao se bachna chahiye)

---

## 3. 💻 5 Computer Science Sentences (ACTIVE VOICE)

* Developers detect bias in training data. (Developers training data mein bias ko detect karte hain)
* Engineers improve models to reduce bias. (Engineers models ko behtar banate hain taake bias kam ho)
* The system learns bias from historical data. (System purane data se bias seekhta hai)
* Researchers analyze bias in AI outputs. (Researchers AI outputs mein bias ko analyze karte hain)
* Programmers test algorithms for bias. (Programmers algorithms ko bias ke liye test karte hain)

---

## 4. 💻 5 Computer Science Sentences (PASSIVE VOICE)

* Bias is detected in the training dataset by developers. (Bias ko training dataset mein developers ke zariye detect kiya jata hai)
* The model is improved to reduce bias by engineers. (Model ko engineers ke zariye behtar banaya jata hai taake bias kam ho)
* Bias is learned from historical data by the system. (Bias system ke zariye purane data se seekha jata hai)
* Bias is analyzed in AI outputs by researchers. (Bias AI outputs mein researchers ke zariye analyze kiya jata hai)
* Algorithms are tested for bias by programmers. (Algorithms ko programmers ke zariye bias ke liye test kiya jata hai)
