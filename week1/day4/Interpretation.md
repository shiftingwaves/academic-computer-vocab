## 📘 Word: **Interpretation**

---

## 1. 🧠 Basic Meaning

**Interpretation** means:
👉 understanding or explaining the meaning of something
(itna samajhna ke kisi cheez ka matlab kya hai)

---

## 2. 🇵🇰 Roman Urdu Meaning

**Interpretation = kisi cheez ka matlab samajhna ya explain karna** (kisi cheez ko samajh kar uska matlab nikalna)

---

## 3. ⭐ Why This Word Matters

We use interpretation when:

* we read information
* we solve problems
* we understand data or results

Without interpretation, we only see things but don’t understand them (warna hum sirf dekhte hain lekin samajh nahi aata).

---

## 4. 🌍 Daily-Life Usage

We interpret many things in daily life:

* a friend’s message
* traffic signs
* exam questions

---

## 5. 🎓 Academic Usage

In studies, interpretation means:

* explaining ideas from books
* understanding exam questions
* analyzing results

---

## 6. 💻 Computer Science Usage

In Computer Science:

* computers **interpret code**
* data must be interpreted to understand results
* users interpret output from programs

---

## 7. 👶 Beginner Explanation

Interpretation is like:
👉 “reading something and understanding what it really means” (jaise kisi cheez ko dekh kar uska asal matlab samajhna)

---

## 8. 🔄 Synonyms vs Similar Words

**Synonyms:**

* explanation
* understanding
* analysis

**Similar words:**

* translation (language change)
* decoding (code samajhna)

---

## 9. ⛔ Antonyms

* confusion
* misunderstanding
* misinterpretation (galat samajhna)

---

## 10. 🌱 Word Family

* interpret (verb)
* interpreter (person who explains/translates)
* interpretation (noun)
* interpretive (adjective)

---

## 11. 💬 Common Phrases

* correct interpretation
* data interpretation
* interpretation of results
* personal interpretation

---

## 12. 🧠 Memory Technique

Think:
👉 “INTER + PRET = inside meaning try karna”
So interpretation = meaning ko andar se samajhna (andar se matlab nikalna)

---

## 13. ⚠️ Common Mistakes Students Make

* Thinking it means only “translation” (sirf translation nahi hota, understanding hoti hai)
* Confusing interpretation with “writing”
* Ignoring context (same sentence ka different meaning ho sakta hai)

---

# ✍️ Sentences

---

## 🏠 1. Daily-Life Sentences

* I gave my interpretation of his message. (Main ne uske message ka matlab apne hisaab se samjha)
* Her interpretation of the joke was different. (Us ne joke ko thoda alag tareeke se samjha)
* The teacher asked for my interpretation of the story. (Teacher ne mujh se story ka matlab poocha)

---

## 🎓 2. Academic Sentences

* The interpretation of the poem was discussed in class. (Class mein poem ka matlab discuss hua)
* His interpretation of the theory was incorrect. (Us ka theory samajhne ka tareeqa galat tha)
* Students wrote their interpretation of the experiment results. (Students ne experiment ke results ka matlab likha)

---

## 💻 3. Computer Science Sentences (ACTIVE VOICE)

* The program interprets user input in real time. (Program user ka input foran samajhta hai)
* Developers interpret error messages to fix bugs. (Developers error messages samajh kar bugs theek karte hain)
* The system interprets data from sensors. (System sensors ka data samajhta hai)
* The compiler interprets the source code step by step. (Compiler code ko step by step samajhta hai)
* The AI model interprets patterns in data. (AI model data ke patterns samajhta hai)

---

## ⚙️ 4. Computer Science Sentences (PASSIVE VOICE)

* User input is interpreted by the program. (User input program ke zariye samjha jata hai)
* Error messages are interpreted by developers. (Error messages developers ke through samjhe jate hain)
* Data from sensors is interpreted by the system. (Sensors ka data system ke zariye samjha jata hai)
* Source code is interpreted by the compiler. (Source code compiler ke through samjha jata hai)
* Patterns in data are interpreted by the AI model. (Data ke patterns AI model ke zariye samjhe jate hain)
