# Word: **toward**

## 1. Basic Meaning

**Toward** means:

* in the direction of something
* moving closer to something
* showing a feeling or action for someone/something

(Roman Urdu: kisi cheez ki taraf ya us ki direction mein)

---

## 2. Roman Urdu Meaning

* **taraf**
* **ki direction mein**
* **qareeb hone ki taraf**

Example:

* He walked toward the gate.
  (Woh gate ki taraf chala)

---

## 3. Why This Word Matters

This word is very common in:

* speaking English
* university books
* programming explanations
* academic writing

You will often see:

* move toward
* step toward
* progress toward

(Roman Urdu: yeh lafz rozana English aur studies mein bohat use hota hai)

---

## 4. Daily-Life Usage

We use **toward** when talking about:

* movement
* goals
* feelings

Example:

* She came toward me.
  (Woh meri taraf aayi)

---

## 5. Academic Usage

In studies, **toward** is used for:

* progress
* research direction
* learning goals

Example:

* Students work toward better grades.
  (Students achay numbers hasil karne ki koshish karte hain)

---

## 6. Computer Science Usage

In Computer Science, **toward** is used for:

* moving data
* system improvement
* algorithm progress
* AI development

Example:

* The company is moving toward AI systems.
  (Company AI systems ki taraf ja rahi hai)

---

## 7. Beginner Explanation

Think of **toward** as:

➡️ “going in a direction”

Example:

* The robot moved toward the box.
  (Robot box ki taraf gaya)

---

## 8. Synonyms vs Similar Words

| Word        | Meaning                |
| ----------- | ---------------------- |
| **toward**  | in the direction of    |
| **to**      | going somewhere        |
| **towards** | same meaning as toward |
| **near**    | close to               |

### Small Difference

* **toward/towards** → direction
* **near** → position

Example:

* He walked toward the class.
  (Woh class ki taraf chala)

* He stood near the class.
  (Woh class ke paas khara tha)

---

## 9. Antonyms

Opposite words:

* **away from**
* **far from**

Example:

* The car moved away from the building.
  (Gaari building se door chali gayi)
