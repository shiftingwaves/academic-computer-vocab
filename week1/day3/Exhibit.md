## 📘 Word: **Exhibit**

---

## 1. ✅ Basic Meaning

**Exhibit** means to **show something publicly** or **display something for others to see**.
(Exhibit ka matlab hai kisi cheez ko sab ke samnay dikhana ya display karna)

---

## 2. 🇵🇰 Roman Urdu Meaning

**Exhibit = dikhana / numayan karna / display karna**
(kisi cheez ko logon ke samnay zahir karna)

---

## 3. 🎯 Why This Word Matters

This word is important because we often:

* show information
* display results
* present data in studies and technology
  (yeh word is liye important hai kyun ke hum data aur information ko present karte hain)

---

## 4. 🌍 Daily-Life Usage

We use “exhibit” when showing things in real life:

* art shows
* museum items
* school projects
  (hum yeh word tab use karte hain jab hum kuch logon ko dikhate hain)

---

## 5. 🎓 Academic Usage

In studies, “exhibit” is used when:

* showing charts
* presenting research
* displaying findings
  (academic kaam mein results aur data ko dikhane ke liye use hota hai)

---

## 6. 💻 Computer Science Usage

In Computer Science, “exhibit” is used when:

* software exhibits behavior
* system exhibits errors
* program exhibits output patterns
  (CS mein program ka behavior ya output dikhane ke liye use hota hai)

---

## 7. 🧠 Beginner Explanation

Think of “exhibit” as:
👉 “to show something clearly to others”
(simple idea: kuch cheez sab ko dikhana)

---

## 8. 🔁 Synonyms vs Similar Words

**Synonyms:**

* show
* display
* present
* reveal

**Similar words (but slightly different):**

* demonstrate (samjha kar dikhana)
* express (feelings dikhana)

---

## 9. ❌ Antonyms

* hide (chupana)
* conceal (chhupana)
* cover (dhakna)

---

## 10. 📚 Word Family

* Exhibit (verb/noun)
* Exhibition (noun) → jahan cheezen display hoti hain
* Exhibitor (person who shows things)
* Exhibited (past form)

---

## 11. 💬 Common Phrases

* exhibit behavior
* exhibit results
* exhibit data
* art exhibit
* exhibit symptoms

---

## 12. 🧠 Memory Technique

Think:
👉 “EX + HIT + BIT”
Imagine: “You HIT the screen and it SHOWS something”
So “exhibit” = show on screen or in real life

---

## 13. ⚠️ Common Mistakes Students Make

* Using it like “exit” (wrong word confusion)
* Using it without object (must show WHAT is exhibited)
* Thinking it only means museum display (it is also used in CS and academics)

---

# ✍️ Sentences

---

## 🌍 1. Daily-Life Sentences

* I exhibit my drawings in school. (Main apni drawings school mein dikhata hoon)
* The museum exhibits old coins. (Museum purane sikke display karta hai)
* She exhibits her project at the fair. (Woh apna project fair mein dikhati hai)

---

## 🎓 2. Academic Sentences

* The student exhibits strong understanding of mathematics. (Student maths ki achi samajh dikhata hai)
* The report exhibits clear research findings. (Report research ke clear results dikhata hai)
* The chart exhibits data trends over time. (Chart time ke sath data ka pattern dikhata hai)

---

## 💻 3. Computer Science Sentences (ACTIVE VOICE)

* The program exhibits correct output after execution. (Program run hone ke baad sahi output dikhata hai)
* The system exhibits high performance under load. (System load mein acha performance dikhata hai)
* The algorithm exhibits fast sorting behavior. (Algorithm tez sorting behavior dikhata hai)
* The application exhibits user-friendly design. (App easy design dikhata hai)
* The database exhibits consistent data storage. (Database stable data store dikhata hai)

---

## 💻 4. Computer Science Sentences (PASSIVE VOICE)

* Errors are exhibited by the system during runtime. (Run time par system errors dikhata hai)
* Output is exhibited on the screen by the program. (Program screen par output dikhata hai)
* Performance is exhibited by the application under stress. (Stress mein app performance dikhati hai)
* Data patterns are exhibited in the logs. (Logs mein data patterns nazar aate hain)
* Results are exhibited after processing the input. (Input process hone ke baad results dikhte hain)
