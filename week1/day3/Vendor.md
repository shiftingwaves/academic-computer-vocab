# 📘 Word: **Vendor**

---

## 1. 🟢 Basic Meaning

A **vendor** is a person or company that **sells products or services**.
(“Vendor wo shakhs ya company hoti hai jo cheezen ya services bechti hai”)

---

## 2. 🟡 Roman Urdu Meaning

**Vendor = bechne wala / supplier / dukan daar ya company jo saman ya service deti hai**
(Vendor wo hota hai jo product ya service provide karta hai)

---

## 3. 🎯 Why This Word Matters

In real life and Computer Science, we often buy **software, hardware, and services** from vendors.
(Vendor ka concept IT aur daily life dono mein bohat important hai)

---

## 4. 🏠 Daily-Life Usage

* Shops, mobile sellers, and online stores are all vendors. (Dukanain, mobile bechne wale aur online stores sab vendors hotay hain)
* A fruit vendor sells fruits in the market. (Fal bechne wala bazar mein fruits bechta hai)
* I bought a phone from a vendor. (Main ne ek vendor se phone kharida)

---

## 5. 📚 Academic Usage

* The university purchased books from a publishing vendor. (University ne books ek publishing vendor se khareedi)
* The school selects a uniform vendor every year. (School har saal uniform ke liye vendor select karta hai)
* The lab equipment was supplied by a certified vendor. (Lab ka samaan certified vendor ne diya)

---

## 6. 💻 Computer Science Usage

* A software vendor provides programs like Windows or antivirus tools. (Software vendor Windows jese programs provide karta hai)
* Cloud vendors like AWS host online applications. (Cloud vendors online apps host karte hain)
* The database vendor offers tools to manage data efficiently. (Database vendor data manage karne ke tools deta hai)
* Hardware vendors supply CPUs, RAM, and motherboards. (Hardware vendors parts provide karte hain)
* Developers depend on third-party vendors for APIs. (Developers APIs ke liye external vendors par depend karte hain)

---

## 7. 🧠 Beginner Explanation

Think of a vendor as a **helper who gives you what you need in exchange for money or contract**.
(Vendor ek aisa insaan ya company hoti hai jo aapko cheez ya service deti hai aur badlay mein payment leti hai)

---

## 8. 🔁 Synonyms vs Similar Words

**Synonyms:**

* Supplier
* Seller
* Provider
  (Vendor jesa kaam karne wale lafz)

**Similar Words:**

* Shopkeeper (sirf dukan wala hota hai)
* Distributor (large scale supply karta hai)

---

## 9. ❌ Antonyms

* Buyer (khareedne wala)
* Customer (grahak)
* Consumer (istemal karne wala)

---

## 10. 🌱 Word Family

* Vend (verb: sell)
* Vendor (noun)
* Vendoring (process of selling/providing)
* Vended (past form in formal use)

---

## 11. 💬 Common Phrases

* Software vendor
* Third-party vendor
* Approved vendor
* Vendor list
* Vendor contract

---

## 12. 🧠 Memory Technique

Think: **Vendor = “Sell-er” = “Send-er of products”**
(Vendor wo hai jo cheez aap tak “send” karta hai aur bechta hai)

---

## 13. ⚠️ Common Mistakes Students Make

* Confusing vendor with customer (vendor bechta hai, customer khareedta hai)
* Thinking vendor is only a shopkeeper (vendor company bhi ho sakta hai)
* Using vendor only for physical shops (IT vendors also exist)

---

# ✍️ Sentences

---

## 1. 🏠 Daily-Life Sentences (3)

* I talked to a vendor at the market. (Main ne bazar mein ek vendor se baat ki)
* The vendor sold fresh vegetables today. (Vendor ne aaj taza sabziyan bechi)
* We paid the vendor for the goods. (Hum ne vendor ko samaan ke paise diye)

---

## 2. 📚 Academic Sentences (3)

* The school hired a vendor for cleaning services. (School ne safai ke liye vendor hire kiya)
* The college changed its book vendor this year. (College ne is saal apna book vendor change kiya)
* The lab works with a certified equipment vendor. (Lab certified equipment vendor ke saath kaam karta hai)

---

## 3. 💻 Computer Science Sentences (ACTIVE VOICE) (5)

* The company selects a cloud vendor for hosting services. (Company hosting ke liye cloud vendor select karti hai)
* Developers integrate APIs from different vendors. (Developers different vendors ki APIs integrate karte hain)
* The IT team evaluates hardware vendors carefully. (IT team hardware vendors ko carefully check karti hai)
* The system uses a third-party vendor for authentication. (System authentication ke liye third-party vendor use karta hai)
* Students study how vendors provide software solutions. (Students study karte hain ke vendors software solutions kaise dete hain)

---

## 4. 💻 Computer Science Sentences (PASSIVE VOICE) (5)

* Cloud services are provided by multiple vendors. (Cloud services multiple vendors ke through provide hoti hain)
* APIs are supplied by external vendors. (APIs external vendors ke zariye supply hoti hain)
* Software tools are delivered by licensed vendors. (Software tools licensed vendors se deliver hotay hain)
* Hardware components are manufactured by different vendors. (Hardware parts different vendors ke through banaye jate hain)
* Security systems are maintained by trusted vendors. (Security systems trusted vendors ke zariye maintain kiye jate hain)
