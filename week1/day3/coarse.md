## 📘 Word: **coarse**

---

## 1. 🧠 Basic Meaning

**Coarse** means something that is **rough, not smooth, or not fine in quality**.
(yaani aisi cheez jo smooth ya delicate na ho, thori rough ho)

---

## 2. 🇵🇰 Roman Urdu Meaning

**Coarse = khurdura / rough / bad quality ya mota texture wala**
(yaani jab koi cheez smooth na ho aur haath par rough feel ho)

---

## 3. ⭐ Why This Word Matters

This word helps you describe:

* quality of materials
* texture (smooth or rough)
* data or results in computer science

(ye word cheezon ki quality aur smoothness explain karne ke liye use hota hai)

---

## 4. 🌍 Daily-Life Usage

You use “coarse” when talking about:

* sand
* cloth
* food texture

(rozmarra zindagi mein jab cheez rough ho ya fine na ho)

---

## 5. 🎓 Academic Usage

In studies, “coarse” is used for:

* coarse analysis (basic study)
* coarse materials in science

(academic level par jab detail kam ho aur general study ho)

---

## 6. 💻 Computer Science Usage

In CS, “coarse” is often used in:

* data representation
* clustering
* parallel processing

(yaani jab system detailed level par na ja kar general level par kaam kare)

---

## 7. 🧑‍🏫 Beginner Explanation

Think of “coarse” like:

* sugar vs salt
  Sugar is fine, salt is more coarse sometimes
  (yaani ek cheez smooth ho aur doosri thori rough ya moti ho)

---

## 8. 🔁 Synonyms vs Similar Words

**Synonyms:**

* rough
* uneven
* harsh
* gritty

**Similar Words:**

* hard (not same meaning)
* thick (sometimes related)

---

## 9. ❌ Antonyms

* smooth
* fine
* soft
* delicate

---

## 10. 📚 Word Family

* coarse (adjective)
* coarsely (adverb)
* coarseness (noun)

---

## 11. 💬 Common Phrases

* coarse sand
* coarse language
* coarse texture
* coarse data

---

## 12. 🧠 Memory Technique

Think:
**“Coarse = Cracked surface”**
(cracked ya tootay huay surface ki tarah rough)

---

## 13. ⚠️ Common Mistakes Students Make

* Confusing **coarse** with **course**
* Thinking it means “bad” only (it mainly means rough, not just bad)
* Using it for emotions instead of texture or quality
