# 📘 Word: **likelihood**

## 1. Basic Meaning

**Likelihood** means the **chance** that something will happen.
(Is ka matlab hai kisi cheez ke hone ka chance)

---

## 2. Roman Urdu Meaning

**Likelihood = imkaan, hone ka chance, mumkin honay ki surat**
(Yani kisi kaam ke hone ke chances)

---

## 3. Why This Word Matters

This word is very useful in:

* English speaking
* Academic writing
* Research
* Computer Science
* Data analysis
* AI and Machine Learning

(Ye word research aur CS mein bohat use hota hai)

---

We use **likelihood** when talking about:

(Ye aam zindagi mein possibilities batane ke liye use hota hai)

## 7. Beginner Explanation

If something has a **high likelihood**, it will probably happen.
(Agar likelihood zyada ho to kaam hone ke chances zyada hote hain)

If something has a **low likelihood**, it may not happen.
(Agar likelihood kam ho to kaam shayad na ho)

---

## 12. Memory Technique

Think:

**Likelihood → likely → probably happening**

Example:
“If rain is likely, rain may happen.”
(Agar barish likely hai to barish ho sakti hai)

So:
**likelihood = chance of happening**
(Yani hone ka chance)

---

## 13. Common Mistakes Students Make

❌ “Likelihood means guarantee.”
✅ No — it only means **chance**, not certainty.
(Yani pakka nahi hota sirf chance hota hai)

❌ “Likelihood” and “probability” are always the same.
✅ Probability is more mathematical.
(Probability zyada maths wala word hai)
