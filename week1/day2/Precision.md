# **Word: Precision**

## 1. Basic Meaning

**Precision** means doing something very accurately and carefully.

## 2. Roman Urdu Meaning

**Precision** = exactness / bohat sahi aur theek kaam karna *(bohat zyada sahi-pan)*

---

## 3. Why This Word Matters

* This word is important in:

  * studies
  * programming
  * mathematics
  * engineering
  * data analysis

* In Computer Science, small mistakes can create big problems.
  So, **precision** is very important. *(Computer Science mein choti ghalti bhi bara masla bana sakti hai)*

---

## 4. Daily-Life Usage

* We use precision when:

  * measuring medicine
  * cutting paper
  * cooking
  * drawing lines
  * playing games

---

## 5. Academic Usage

* Teachers use this word in:

  * mathematics
  * physics
  * research
  * lab work

* Accurate answers need precision. *(Sahi jawabon ke liye precision zaroori hoti hai)*

---

## 6. Computer Science Usage

* In CS, precision is used in:

  * floating-point numbers
  * calculations
  * machine learning
  * graphics
  * algorithms
  * data processing

* Computers need precision for correct results. *(Computers ko sahi results ke liye precision chahiye hoti hai)*

---

## 7. Beginner Explanation

Imagine you draw a straight line exactly on the ruler mark.
That careful accuracy is called **precision**. *(Yani bilkul sahi tareeqay se kaam karna)*

---

---

## 13. Common Mistakes Students Make

❌ Confusing **precision** with **speed**

* Fast work is not always precise.

❌ Thinking precision means only “correct”

* Precision also means detailed and exact.

❌ Wrong pronunciation

* Correct: *pri-si-zhun*
