# 📘 Word: **arbitrary**

## 1. Basic Meaning

**Arbitrary** means something chosen **without a clear reason**, rule, or system.
(Arbitrary ka matlab hai baghair kisi khaas reason ya rule ke faisla karna)

---

## 2. Roman Urdu Meaning

**Arbitrary = Baghair reason ke / Random tareeqe se**

---

## 3. Why This Word Matters

* This word is common in:

  * Programming
  * Mathematics
  * AI
  * Research
  * Daily decisions

* In Computer Science, programmers sometimes use **arbitrary values** or **arbitrary limits**.

---

## 4. Daily-Life Usage

* A teacher may choose an arbitrary seat number.
* Someone may set an arbitrary rule at home.
* A game may use arbitrary points.

---

## 5. Academic Usage

* Researchers avoid arbitrary decisions.
* Scientists explain why they choose values.
* Good research needs logical choices.

---

## 6. Computer Science Usage

* Programmers use arbitrary numbers in testing.
* An algorithm may have an arbitrary limit.
* Databases may store arbitrary data sizes.
* AI systems should avoid arbitrary decisions.

---

## 7. Beginner Explanation

Imagine your friend says:

👉 “I will pick number 7.”

But he gives **no reason**.

That choice is **arbitrary**.

It means:

* not based on logic
* not based on rules
* just chosen freely

---

* Picking a random password length like 37.

## 13. Common Mistakes Students Make

❌ Wrong:

* “The answer is arbitrary correct.”

✅ Correct:

* “The teacher made an arbitrary decision.”

❌ Wrong:

* “Arbitrary means accurate.”

✅ Correct:

* “Arbitrary means without a fixed reason.”
