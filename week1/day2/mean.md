# **Word: mean (adjective)**

## 1. Basic Meaning

**Mean** (adjective) describes a person who is **unkind, rude, or not nice**.

---

## 2. Roman Urdu Meaning

A mean person hurts others with words or actions. (**bura ya badtameez**)

---

## 3. Why This Word Matters

* People use this word in school, daily talk, movies, and social media.
* In English conversations, you will hear:

  * “Don’t be mean.”
  * “That was mean.”
* It helps you describe behavior.

---

## 4. Daily-Life Usage

You can use **mean** when someone:

* says hurtful words
* behaves badly
* does not help others
* laughs at someone

Example:

* “The boy was mean to his friend.”

---

## 5. Academic Usage

In school or university:

* teachers may say students should not be mean
* you can describe characters in essays
* you can discuss behavior in psychology or communication

Example:

* “The character in the story was mean.”

---

## 6. Computer Science Usage

In CS, we usually do **not** use “mean” for behavior.
But sometimes programmers say:

* “The user interface is mean to beginners.”
* “The game AI becomes mean at higher levels.”

This means the system behaves harshly or unfairly.

---

## 7. Beginner Explanation

If someone:

* hurts feelings
* speaks badly
* acts cruelly

then we call them **mean**.

Simple idea:

> **Mean = not kind**

---

## 8. Synonyms vs Similar Words

| Word       | Meaning               |
| ---------- | --------------------- |
| **mean**   | not kind              |
| **rude**   | bad manners           |
| **unkind** | not caring            |
| **cruel**  | very hurtful          |
| **harsh**  | very strict or strong |

---

## 9. Antonyms

| Word         | Meaning          |
| ------------ | ---------------- |
| **kind**     | caring           |
| **nice**     | pleasant         |
| **friendly** | good with people |
| **gentle**   | soft and caring  |

---

## 10. Word Family

| Word         | Type      |
| ------------ | --------- |
| **mean**     | adjective |
| **meanly**   | adverb    |
| **meanness** | noun      |

---

## 11. Common Phrases

* **mean person**
* **mean behavior**
* **don’t be mean**
* **that was mean**
* **mean comment**

---

## 12. Memory Technique

Think:

> **Mean people make others feel bad.**

“Mean” sounds like:

* “meen” → imagine a person saying hurtful things.

---

## 13. Common Mistakes Students Make

❌ “He is very meanly.”
✅ “He is very mean.”

❌ “My teacher mean.”
✅ “My teacher is mean.”

❌ Using “mean” instead of “meaning”
✅ “What does this word mean?” (verb)
✅ “He is mean.” (adjective)

# 1. Easy Daily-Life Sentences

1. The boy was mean to his sister.
   (Woh larka apni behen ke saath bura tha)

2. Please do not be mean to animals.
   (Barah-e-karam janwaron ke saath bura mat bano)

3. Her comment was very mean.
   (Us ka comment bohat bura tha)

---

# 2. Academic Sentences

1. The student wrote a mean message in class.
   (Student ne class mein bura message likha)

2. The story shows a mean character.
   (Kahani mein ek bura kirdar dikhaya gaya hai)

3. Good students should not be mean to others.
   (Achay students ko dusron ke saath bura nahi hona chahiye)

---

# 3. Computer Science Sentences (ACTIVE VOICE)

1. The game AI gives mean replies to new players.
   (Game AI naye players ko bure jawab deta hai)

2. The chatbot sends mean messages during testing.
   (Chatbot testing ke dauran bure messages bhejta hai)

3. The online player writes mean comments in the chat.
   (Online player chat mein bure comments likhta hai)

4. The system shows a mean warning to the user.
   (System user ko bura warning message dikhata hai)

5. The moderator removes mean posts from the forum.
   (Moderator forum se bure posts hata deta hai)

---

# 4. Computer Science Sentences (PASSIVE VOICE)

1. Mean messages are detected by the software.
   (Bure messages software ke zariye pehchane jate hain)

2. Mean comments are removed from the database.
   (Bure comments database se hata diye jate hain)

3. Mean behavior is reported by online users.
   (Bure rawaiye ki report online users ke zariye ki jati hai)

4. Mean usernames are blocked by the system.
   (Bure usernames system ke zariye block kiye jate hain)

5. Mean text is filtered by the chat application.
   (Bura text chat application ke zariye filter kiya jata hai)
