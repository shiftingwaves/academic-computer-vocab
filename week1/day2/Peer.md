## 📘 Word: **Peer**

---

## 1. ✅ Basic Meaning

A **peer** means a person who is **equal to you in age, level, or rank**.
It is someone in the same group as you.
(yaani aap ke barabar level ya umar ka insaan)

---

## 2. 🇵🇰 Roman Urdu Meaning

**Peer = barabar ka insaan / hum level dost ya student**
(jo aap ke saath same class, same age ya same level par ho)

---

## 3. ⭐ Why This Word Matters

In studies and computer science, you often work with **peers**.
They help you learn, share ideas, and solve problems together.
(kyun ke classmates ya group members se seekhna bohat important hota hai)

---

## 4. 🏡 Daily-Life Usage

* We talk to our peers in school. (Hum school mein apne barabar walon se baat karte hain)
* My peers help me in homework. (Mere hum class mujhe homework mein madad karte hain)
* I play games with my peers. (Main apne doston ke saath games khelta hoon)

---

## 5. 🎓 Academic Usage

* Students learn better with their peers. (Students apne class fellows ke saath behtar seekhte hain)
* Peers discuss topics in groups. (Students groups mein topics discuss karte hain)
* Teachers encourage peer learning. (Teachers students ko group learning karwate hain)

---

## 6. 💻 Computer Science Usage

* Developers review code with their peers. (Developers apne barabar programmers ke saath code check karte hain)
* Peer programming helps beginners learn coding faster. (Peer programming se beginners coding jaldi seekhte hain)
* Peers share algorithms in study groups. (Students algorithms apne group mein share karte hain)
* Peer feedback improves software quality. (Peer feedback software ko behtar banata hai)
* Programmers solve bugs with their peers. (Programmers bugs apne doston ke saath solve karte hain)

---

## 7. 🧠 Beginner Explanation

Think of **peers** as your **classmates or friends at the same level**.
They are not teachers or juniors—just people like you.
(yaani jo aap ke jese same level par hon, na upar na neeche)

---

## 8. 🔁 Synonyms vs Similar Words

**Synonyms (similar meaning):**

* Classmate
* Colleague
* Teammate

**Similar but not exact:**

* Friend (friend zaroori nahi same level ho)
* Mentor (mentor higher level hota hai)

---

## 9. ⛔ Antonyms

* Teacher
* Senior
* Expert
* Leader

---

## 10. 🌱 Word Family

* Peer (noun)
* Peer group (group of same level people)
* Peer review (checking work by peers)
* Peer learning (learning with peers)

---

## 11. 💬 Common Phrases

* Peer group
* Peer pressure
* Peer review
* Peer learning
* Peer support

---

## 12. 🧠 Memory Technique

Think: **“PEER = People Equal in Education/Experience Rank”**
Ya yaad rakhein:
**Peer = same level friend**
(yaani jo aap jesa hi ho)

---

## 13. ⚠️ Common Mistakes Students Make

* ❌ Thinking peer means only “friend”
  ✔ It means someone **same level**, not just friend

* ❌ Confusing peer with teacher
  ✔ Teacher is higher level, peer is equal level

* ❌ Using peer for younger students
  ✔ Peer is not junior or senior, it is equal
