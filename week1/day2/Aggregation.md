# **Aggregation**

## 1. Basic Meaning

**Aggregation** means collecting many things and bringing them together into one group.
(Main cheezon ko ikatha karke aik group banana)

---

## 2. Roman Urdu Meaning

* **Aggregation = cheezon ko mila kar aik jagah jama karna**
* **Aggregation = data ko combine karna**
* **Aggregation = information ko ikatha karna**

---

## 3. Why This Word Matters

This word is very important in:

* Data Science
* Databases
* Programming
* Business reports
* Research

Computer Science students often see this word in:

* SQL
* Data analysis
* OOP (Object-Oriented Programming)
* Algorithms

(Is lafz ka istemal CS aur data wali fields mein bohat hota hai)

---

## 4. Daily-Life Usage

We use aggregation when:

* We collect money
* We gather information
* We combine results
* We make reports from many records

(Jab alag alag cheezain mila kar aik result banaya jaye)

---

## 5. Academic Usage

In studies, aggregation means:

* combining data
* collecting research results
* summarizing information

(Research ya data ko mila kar summary banana)

---

## 6. Computer Science Usage

In Computer Science, aggregation is used for:

* Combining data records
* Database functions
* OOP relationships
* Analytics systems

(Data aur objects ko logically combine karna)

---

## 7. Beginner Explanation

Imagine:

* 10 students give marks
* Teacher combines all marks into one report

This process is called **aggregation**.

(Yani bohat si information ko mila kar aik output banana)

Think:

(Jab bohat si choti cheezain aik jagah jama ho jayein)

---

## 13. Common Mistakes Students Make

### ❌ Mistake 1:

Using aggregation for only one item.

Aggregation always involves **many things**.
(Aggregation aik se zyada cheezon ke liye hoti hai)

### ❌ Mistake 2:

Confusing aggregation with sorting.

* Sorting = arrange karna
* Aggregation = combine karna

(Dono ka meaning alag hai)
