## 📘 Word: **Greedy**

---

## 1. 🧠 Basic Meaning

**Greedy** means wanting **more and more**, even when you already have enough.
(Itna zyada chahna ke kabhi satisfy na hona)

---

## 2. 🗣️ Roman Urdu Meaning

**Greedy = lalchi / zyada chahne wala**
(Jo hamesha zyada cheezain lena chahta hai aur kabhi khush nahi hota)

---

## 3. ⭐ Why This Word Matters

This word is important because it is used in:

* behavior of people
* stories and morals
* Computer Science algorithms (very important topic)

---

## 4. 🌍 Daily-Life Usage

We use it to describe people or habits.

---

## 5. 📚 Academic Usage

Used in essays, psychology, and ethics to describe behavior:

* human attitude
* decision making
* moral lessons

---

## 6. 💻 Computer Science Usage

In CS, **Greedy** is a type of algorithm:

* It chooses the **best option at each step**
* It does NOT think about future consequences

---

## 7. 👶 Beginner Explanation

A greedy person or algorithm always says:
👉 “I want the biggest, fastest, or best thing right now!”

---

## 8. 🔁 Synonyms vs Similar Words

**Synonyms:**

* selfish
* hungry for more
* money-minded

**Similar words:**

* ambitious (positive sometimes)
* eager (not always negative)

---

## 9. ❌ Antonyms

* generous
* kind
* satisfied
* content

---

## 10. 🌱 Word Family

* Greed (noun)
* Greedy (adjective)
* Greedily (adverb)

---

## 11. 💬 Common Phrases

* greedy person
* greedy algorithm
* greedy for money
* greedy behavior

---

## 12. 🧠 Memory Technique

Think of a person eating all pizza alone and not sharing 🍕
👉 “He is so greedy!”
(yaani woh sab khud rakh raha hai)

---

## 13. ⚠️ Common Mistakes Students Make

* Thinking greedy always means “bad person” (not always in CS)
* Mixing “greedy algorithm” with “greedy human behavior”
* Translating word-for-word instead of meaning

---

# ✍️ SENTENCES

---

## 🌍 1. Daily-Life Sentences

* He is greedy and never shares his food. (Woh lalchi hai aur kabhi apna khana share nahi karta)
* Don’t be greedy when you have enough money. (Jab paisay kaafi hon to lalchi nahi hona chahiye)
* The greedy boy took all the chocolates. (Lalchi larke ne sari chocolates le li)

---

## 📚 2. Academic Sentences

* Greedy behavior can affect group decision making. (Lalchi rawaiya group ke faislon ko affect karta hai)
* The story teaches us not to be greedy. (Yeh kahani humein lalach se bachne ka sabaq deti hai)
* Greedy choices often lead to unfair results. (Lalchi faislay aksar na-insafi paida karte hain)

---

## 💻 3. Computer Science Sentences (ACTIVE VOICE)

* The algorithm selects the **greedy choice** at each step. (Algorithm har step par lalchi choice select karta hai)
* The programmer designs a greedy algorithm for optimization. (Programmer optimization ke liye greedy algorithm design karta hai)
* We use greedy strategy to solve scheduling problems. (Hum scheduling problems solve karne ke liye greedy strategy use karte hain)
* The system applies a greedy method to improve performance. (System performance improve karne ke liye greedy method apply karta hai)
* Developers test greedy algorithms for efficiency. (Developers greedy algorithms ko efficiency ke liye test karte hain)

---

## 💻 4. Computer Science Sentences (PASSIVE VOICE)

* A greedy choice is selected by the algorithm at each step. (Har step par greedy choice algorithm ke zariye select hoti hai)
* A greedy approach is used in many optimization problems. (Greedy approach kai optimization problems mein use hoti hai)
* The scheduling problem is solved using a greedy technique. (Scheduling problem greedy technique se solve ki jati hai)
* The solution is generated by a greedy algorithm. (Solution greedy algorithm ke zariye generate hota hai)
* Efficiency is improved by applying greedy strategies. (Efficiency greedy strategies apply karne se improve hoti hai)
