# 📘 Word: **partial**

## 1. Basic Meaning

**Partial** means **not complete** or **only a part of something**.
(Partial ka matlab hai kisi cheez ka poora na hona)

---

## 2. Roman Urdu Meaning

**Partial = adhoora / kuch hissa**

---

## 3. Why This Word Matters

This word is very common in:

* Daily conversation
* Exams
* Computer Science
* Programming
* Mathematics
* Research papers

You will often hear:

* partial result
* partial data
* partial success
* partial function

---

## 4. Daily-Life Usage

We use **partial** when something is incomplete.

Examples:

* partial payment
* partial information
* partial work

---

## 5. Academic Usage

In studies, teachers use this word for:

* partial marks
* partial answer
* partial solution

---

## 6. Computer Science Usage

In Computer Science, **partial** is used for:

* partial data
* partial output
* partial matching
* partial functions
* partial loading

Example:
A program may show **partial results** before finishing.

---

## 7. Beginner Explanation

Imagine you eat only half of a pizza.

You did not eat the full pizza.
You ate a **partial** pizza.

So:

* Full = complete
* Partial = incomplete

---

## 8. Synonyms vs Similar Words

| Word       | Meaning        |
| ---------- | -------------- |
| incomplete | not finished   |
| unfinished | still not done |
| limited    | small amount   |
| half       | one part only  |

### Difference:

* **Partial** = some part exists
* **Complete** = everything exists

---

## 9. Antonyms

| Word     | Meaning    |
| -------- | ---------- |
| complete | fully done |
| full     | whole      |
| total    | everything |
| entire   | all parts  |

---

Think:

If only a **part** is done, it is **partial**.

Easy memory trick:

# 📘 1. **partial** (Adjective)

## ✅ Meaning

Something that is **not complete** or **only a part**.
(Kisi cheez ka adhoora ya kuch hissa)

## ✅ Simple Explanation

If something is not fully done, it is **partial**.
(Agar koi cheez poori na ho to usay partial kehte hain)

## ✍️ Example Sentences

* The work is partial. (Kaam adhoora hai)
* We received partial data. (Hamein kuch data mila)

---

## 🔹 Passive Voice Sentences

### Present Simple Passive

* Partial data is stored in the system. (Adhoora data system mein store kiya jata hai)

### Past Simple Passive

* Partial marks were given to the students. (Students ko adhoore marks diye gaye)

### Future Simple Passive

* Partial results will be shown tomorrow. (Adhoore results kal dikhaye jayenge)

---

# 📘 2. **partially** (Adverb)

## ✅ Meaning

Something is done **not fully**, only a little or some part.
(Koi kaam poori tarah nahi balkay kuch hissa tak hona)

## ✅ Simple Explanation

If work is half done, it is done **partially**.
(Agar kaam adha ho to woh partially hota hai)

## ✍️ Example Sentences

* The file loaded partially. (File kuch hissa load hui)
* I partially completed the task. (Main ne kaam kuch hissa tak mukammal kiya)

---

## 🔹 Passive Voice Sentences

### Present Simple Passive

* The project is partially managed by AI. (Project kuch hissa AI ke zariye manage kiya jata hai)

### Past Simple Passive

* The system was partially updated yesterday. (System ko kal kuch hissa tak update kiya gaya)

### Future Simple Passive

* The website will be partially redesigned. (Website ko kuch hissa tak dobara design kiya jayega)

---

# 📘 3. **impartial** (Adjective)

## ✅ Meaning

Fair and neutral; not supporting one side.
(Bila tarafdari ya insaaf se)

## ✅ Simple Explanation

An impartial person treats everyone equally.
(Impartial shakhs sab ke sath barabar sulook karta hai)

## ✍️ Example Sentences

* The judge is impartial. (Judge bila tarafdaar hai)
* The teacher gave an impartial opinion. (Teacher ne insaaf wali raye di)

---

## 🔹 Passive Voice Sentences

### Present Simple Passive

* An impartial decision is made by the committee. (Committee ke zariye insaaf wala faisla kiya jata hai)

### Past Simple Passive

* An impartial review was written by the professor. (Professor ke zariye bila tarafdaar review likha gaya)

### Future Simple Passive

* An impartial report will be prepared next week. (Bila tarafdaar report agle hafte tayar ki jayegi)

---

# 📘 4. **impartiality** (Noun)

## ✅ Meaning

The quality of being fair and neutral.
(Bila tarafdari ki sifat)

## ✅ Simple Explanation

Impartiality means fairness to everyone.
(Impartiality ka matlab sab ke sath insaaf karna hai)

## ✍️ Example Sentences

* The manager showed impartiality. (Manager ne insaaf dikhaya)
* Impartiality is important in exams. (Exams mein bila tarafdari zaroori hai)

---

## 🔹 Passive Voice Sentences

### Present Simple Passive

* Impartiality is respected in courts. (Courts mein bila tarafdari ki izzat ki jati hai)

### Past Simple Passive

* Impartiality was maintained during the meeting. (Meeting ke dauran bila tarafdari barqarar rakhi gayi)

### Future Simple Passive

* Impartiality will be promoted in the organization. (Organization mein bila tarafdari ko farogh diya jayega)
