# 📘 Word: **threshold**

## 1. Basic Meaning

A **threshold** is a **limit**, **starting point**, or **level** where something begins or changes.
(Threshold ka matlab hai kisi cheez ki had ya shuru hone wali level)

---

## 2. Roman Urdu Meaning

**Threshold = Had / Limit / Aaghaz ki level**

---

## 3. Why This Word Matters

* This word is common in:

  * Science
  * Computer Science
  * AI
  * Data Analysis
  * Daily life decisions

* In CS, systems often work only after a certain **threshold** is reached.
  (System tab kaam karta hai jab ek khaas limit tak value pohanch jaye)

---

## 4. Daily-Life Usage

* A phone alarm rings after a sound threshold.
* Parents may set a screen-time threshold.
* A bank may have a money threshold for loans.

---

## 5. Academic Usage

* Teachers use score thresholds in exams.
* Researchers set thresholds in experiments.
* Universities set admission thresholds.

---

## 6. Computer Science Usage

* AI models use confidence thresholds.
* Networks use traffic thresholds.
* Programs check memory thresholds.
* Sensors activate after threshold values.

---

## 7. Beginner Explanation

Think of a **threshold** like a line.

* Before the line → nothing happens
* After the line → action starts

Example:

* If temperature goes above 40°C, the fan starts.
* Here, **40°C is the threshold**.
