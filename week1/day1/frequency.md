## 📘 Word: **frequency**

---

## 1. Basic Meaning

**Frequency** means:
👉 how often something happens
👉 number of times something repeats in a period

---

## 2. Roman Urdu Meaning

**Frequency = dafa hone ki tadaad / kitni baar kuch hota hai**

---

## 3. Why This Word Matters

We use this word to measure repetition.
Example: how many times something happens in time or data.

---

## 4. Daily-Life Usage

* How many times you go outside in a week
* How often a bus comes
* How many times a phone rings

---

## 5. Academic Usage

In science and math, frequency means:
👉 number of occurrences in a fixed time

Example:

* waves
* signals
* data counts

---

## 6. Computer Science Usage

In Computer Science, frequency is used in:

* data analysis
* algorithms
* networks
* signal processing

Example:
👉 How many times a value appears in data

---

## 7. Beginner Explanation

Think like this:
If something repeats again and again, we count it.
That count is **frequency**.

---

## 8. Synonyms vs Similar Words

**Synonyms:**

* repetition
* occurrence rate
* count

**Similar words (not exact):**

* speed (not same, but related in signals)
* rate (broader meaning)

---

## 9. Antonyms

* rarity
* infrequency
* never

---

## 10. Word Family

* frequent (adjective)
* frequently (adverb)
* frequency (noun)

---

## 11. Common Phrases

* high frequency
* low frequency
* frequency of events
* frequency distribution

---

## 12. Memory Technique

Think:
👉 “FREE + QUEN + CY”

Now imagine:
A bell ringing again and again = **frequency = repetition count**

---

## 13. Common Mistakes Students Make

❌ Thinking frequency = speed
❌ Mixing it with time
❌ Forgetting it means “count of repetition”
