# Word: **integrate**

## 1. Basic Meaning

**Integrate** means:

* to combine things together
* to connect different parts into one system

Example:

* We integrate the payment system with the app.

---

## 2. Roman Urdu Meaning

**Integrate** =

* *jorna*
* *milana*
* *aik system mein shamil karna*

---

## 3. Why This Word Matters

This word is very important in:

* Computer Science
* Software development
* APIs
* Databases
* University studies

In tech, systems often work together.
That process is called **integration**.

---

## 4. Daily-Life Usage

Examples:

* The school integrated new students into the class.
* I integrated my study schedule with sports time.
* The company integrated online payments.

---

## 5. Academic Usage

Examples:

* The teacher integrated theory with practice.
* The course integrates mathematics and programming.
* Researchers integrate different methods.

---

## 6. Computer Science Usage

Very common in CS.

Examples:

* Developers integrate APIs into apps.
* We integrate the database with the website.
* The software integrates multiple services.

---

## 7. Beginner Explanation

Think like this:

👉 Many separate parts become one working system.

Example:

* Mobile app + payment gateway + database
  All connected together = integrated system

---

## 8. Synonyms vs Similar Words

| Word    | Meaning                   |
| ------- | ------------------------- |
| combine | join things               |
| connect | link together             |
| merge   | mix into one              |
| unite   | bring together            |
| attach  | join physically/digitally |

### Difference

* **Integrate** usually means joining parts so they work smoothly together.

---

## 9. Antonyms

Opposite words:

* separate
* disconnect
* divide
* remove
* isolate

## 13. Common Mistakes Students Make

### ❌ Wrong:

* “I integrate with him yesterday.”

### ✔ Correct:

* “I integrated with him yesterday.”

---

### ❌ Wrong:

* “The app integrate payment system.”

### ✔ Correct:

* “The app integrates the payment system.”

---

### ❌ Wrong:

* “We integrated to the API.”

### ✔ Correct:

* “We integrated the API.”

or

### ✔ Correct:

* “We integrated with the API.”
