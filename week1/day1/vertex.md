## 📘 Word: **vertex**

---

## 1. Basic Meaning

**Vertex** means:
👉 a corner point of a shape
👉 where two lines or edges meet

Example:

* Triangle has 3 vertices
* Square has 4 vertices

---

## 2. Roman Urdu Meaning

**Vertex = kona (corner point)**
(plural: vertices = kone)

---

## 3. Why This Word Matters

We use this word to:

* describe shapes
* understand graphs
* work with networks in Computer Science

Very important in math and programming.

---

## 4. Daily-Life Usage

* Corner of a table
* Corner of a room
* Point where two roads meet

Example:
👉 “The building has many vertices at corners.”

---

## 5. Academic Usage

In mathematics:

* vertex = corner point of geometric shapes
* used in geometry and graphs

---

## 6. Computer Science Usage

In Computer Science:

* graphs have vertices (nodes)
* used in trees, networks, and data structures
* each vertex represents a point or object

---

## 7. Beginner Explanation

Think like this:
If you draw a triangle, each corner is a **vertex**.
If you connect points in a network, each point is a vertex.

---

## 8. Synonyms vs Similar Words

**Synonyms:**

* corner (simple meaning)
* point (in graphs)

**Similar words:**

* node (CS meaning, similar but not same)
* edge (connection between vertices)

---

## 9. Antonyms

* edge (opposite role in graph)
* center (not a corner)

---

## 10. Word Family

* vertices (plural form)
* vertex-based
* vertex point

---

## 11. Common Phrases

* graph vertex
* vertex of a triangle
* adjacent vertices
* vertex set

---

## 12. Memory Technique

Think:
👉 “VERTEX = VERY TOP EDGE POINT”

Imagine:
A triangle → its sharp corner is a **vertex**

---

## 13. Common Mistakes Students Make

❌ Thinking vertex = line
❌ Confusing vertex with edge
❌ Forgetting plural form is vertices
