## 📘 Word: **priority**

---

## 1. Basic Meaning

**Priority** means:
👉 something that is more important than other things
👉 what you do first because it matters most

---

## 2. Roman Urdu Meaning

**Priority = ahmiyat / pehlay number par aane wali cheez**

---

## 3. Why This Word Matters

We use this word to decide:

* what to do first
* what is most important
* how to manage tasks and time

Very important in study, life, and programming.

---

## 4. Daily-Life Usage

* Homework is your priority
* Health is a priority
* Phone messages may have different importance

Example:
👉 “Studying is my first priority.”

---

## 5. Academic Usage

In academics:

* important topics are given priority
* exam preparation depends on priority topics

---

## 6. Computer Science Usage

In Computer Science:

* tasks are executed based on priority
* operating systems use priority scheduling
* important processes run first

---

## 7. Beginner Explanation

Think like this:
If you have 5 tasks, you do the most important one first.
That most important task is your **priority**.

---

## 8. Synonyms vs Similar Words

**Synonyms:**

* importance
* preference
* ranking

**Similar words:**

* urgency (related but not same)
* order (sequence idea)

---

## 9. Antonyms

* unimportant
* low priority
* secondary

---

## 10. Word Family

* prioritize (verb)
* prioritization (noun)
* priority-based

---

## 11. Common Phrases

* top priority
* low priority
* give priority to
* priority task

---

## 12. Memory Technique

Think:
👉 “PRI = first”
👉 “ORITY = order”

So:
**Priority = first important order**

Imagine:
You have 3 tasks → the most important comes first = priority

---

## 13. Common Mistakes Students Make

❌ Thinking all tasks have same importance
❌ Confusing priority with speed
❌ Forgetting priority = “what comes first”
