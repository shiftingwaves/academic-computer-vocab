# Word: **desire**

## 1. Basic Meaning

* **Desire** = a strong **wish** or **want**
* Jab aap kisi cheez ko bohat chahte ho

## 2. Roman Urdu Meaning

* **Desire** = **khwahish**, **zor ki chahat**
* Example:

  * “I desire success.”
  * “Mujhe kamyabi ki khwahish hai.”

---

## 3. Why This Word Matters

* English books mein bohat use hota hai
* Exams aur essays mein common hai
* Motivation aur goals batane ke liye useful hai
* Programming aur AI research papers mein bhi milta hai

---

## 4. Daily-Life Usage

* “She has a desire to travel.”
* “He desires a new phone.”
* “Students desire good marks.”

---

## 5. Academic Usage

* Teachers use it in essays and reports
* Example:

  * “Researchers desire accurate results.”

---

## 6. Computer Science Usage

* Tech goals explain karne ke liye
* Example:

  * “Developers desire secure systems.”
* AI field mein:

  * “Users desire fast applications.”

---

## 7. Beginner Explanation

Socho:

* Aapko game khelne ka bohat mann hai
* Ya programming seekhne ka strong wish hai

Ye strong wish = **desire**

---

# 8. Synonyms vs Similar Words

| Word       | Meaning     | Difference          |
| ---------- | ----------- | ------------------- |
| **desire** | strong wish | zyada powerful      |
| **want**   | normal wish | simple              |
| **wish**   | hope/chahat | soft feeling        |
| **need**   | zaroorat    | important necessity |

### Example

* “I want tea.” → simple
* “I desire success.” → strong feeling

# 13. Common Mistakes Students Make

### ❌ Mistake 1

* “I am desire success.”

### ✔ Correct

* “I desire success.”
* “I have a desire for success.”

---

### ❌ Mistake 2

Using it for small things

* “I desire water.”

Better:

* “I want water.”

---

### ❌ Mistake 3

Wrong preposition

* “desire of success” ❌

Correct:

* “desire for success” ✔

# 3 Easy Sentences

* I **desire** a good job.
  → Mujhe achi job ki khwahish hai.

* She **desires** happiness.
  → Usay khushi chahiye.

* We **desire** success in life.
  → Hum zindagi mein kamyabi chahte hain.

---

# 3 Academic Sentences

* Students **desire** better grades.
  → Students behtar marks ki khwahish rakhte hain.

* Researchers **desire** accurate data.
  → Researchers sahi data chahte hain.

* Many learners **desire** knowledge.
  → Bohat se learners ilm hasil karna chahte hain.

---

# 5 Computer Science Sentences (ACTIVE VOICE)

* Developers **desire** secure networks.
  → Developers mehfooz networks chahte hain.

* Programmers **desire** clean code.
  → Programmers saaf code ki khwahish rakhte hain.

* Data scientists **desire** accurate models.
  → Data scientists sahi models chahte hain.

* Engineers **desire** faster processors.
  → Engineers tez processors chahte hain.

* Users **desire** simple interfaces.
  → Users asaan interfaces pasand karte hain.

---

# 5 Computer Science Sentences (PASSIVE VOICE)

* Secure systems are **desired** by companies.
  → Mehfooz systems companies ki taraf se pasand kiye jate hain.

* Fast algorithms are **desired** by researchers.
  → Tez algorithms researchers chahte hain.

* Reliable databases are **desired** by banks.
  → Reliable databases banks ki taraf se chahiye hote hain.

* Efficient software is **desired** by users.
  → Efficient software users ki taraf se pasand kiya jata hai.

* Advanced AI tools are **desired** by tech firms.
  → Advanced AI tools tech firms chahte hain.
