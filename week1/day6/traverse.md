# 📘 Word: **traverse**

## 1. Basic Meaning

**Traverse** means:
👉 to move across
👉 to travel through
👉 to pass over something

(Roman Urdu: **kisi jagah ko paar karna ya us mein se guzarna**)

---

## 2. Roman Urdu Meaning

**Traverse** =
👉 **paar karna**
👉 **guzarna**
👉 **cross karna**

---

## 3. Why This Word Matters

This word is important in:

* English speaking
* Maps and travel
* Mathematics
* Data Structures & Algorithms

In Computer Science, we often say:

* traverse an array
* traverse a tree
* traverse a graph

(Roman Urdu: **Computer Science mein data ko step by step dekhne ke liye yeh lafz bohat use hota hai**)

---

## 4. Daily-Life Usage

We use **traverse** when someone moves through an area.

Example:

* We traversed the mountain road.
  (**hum pahaari raaste se guzray**)

---

## 5. Academic Usage

In academics, this word is used in:

* geography
* mathematics
* research
* engineering

Example:

* Scientists traversed the desert for research.
  (**scientists research ke liye sehra se guzray**)

---

## 6. Computer Science Usage

In CS, **traverse** means:
👉 visiting each element one by one

Examples:

* traverse an array
* traverse a linked list
* traverse a tree

Example:

* The algorithm traverses the graph.
  (**algorithm graph ke har hissa ko check karta hai**)

---

## 7. Beginner Explanation

Imagine you walk through every room in a house.

That movement = **traverse**

In programming:
👉 checking every item one by one = traverse

(Roman Urdu: **programming mein data ko ek ek karke dekhna traverse kehlata hai**)

---

## 8. Synonyms vs Similar Words

| Word         | Meaning                     |
| ------------ | --------------------------- |
| **traverse** | move through                |
| **cross**    | go from one side to another |
| **travel**   | move from place to place    |
| **scan**     | check carefully             |
| **iterate**  | repeat through items        |
