# 📘 Word: **Robustness**

---

## 1. 📖 Basic Meaning

**Robustness** means the ability of something to stay strong, stable, and work well even in difficult conditions.
(robustness ka matlab hai kisi cheez ka strong aur stable rehna mushkil halat mein bhi)

---

## 2. 🗣️ Roman Urdu Meaning

**Robustness = mazbooti + reliability + har halat mein kaam karne ki ability**
(robustness ka matlab hai system ya cheez ka har situation mein theek kaam karna)

---

## 3. 🌟 Why This Word Matters

Robustness is important because real systems face errors, pressure, and unexpected problems.
A robust system does not easily break.
(ye is liye important hai kyun ke real systems mein problems aati rehti hain aur strong system tab bhi chal jata hai)

---

## 4. 🏠 Daily-Life Usage

We use robustness when talking about strong things like phones, cars, or machines.
(rozmarra zindagi mein hum robustness tab use karte hain jab koi cheez strong ho)

---

## 5. 🎓 Academic Usage

In studies, robustness means how well a method or idea works under different conditions.
(academic level par robustness ka matlab hota hai ke koi method har condition mein kitna reliable hai)

---

## 6. 💻 Computer Science Usage

In Computer Science, robustness means a program can handle errors, wrong input, or system failures without crashing.
(CS mein robustness ka matlab hai program ka error aane par bhi chalna band na hona)

---

## 7. 🧠 Beginner Explanation

Think of robustness like a strong backpack. Even if you put heavy books or rain comes, it still works fine.
(robustness ko aise samjho jaise strong bag jo har situation mein chal jata hai)

---

## 8. 🔁 Synonyms vs Similar Words

* **Reliability** (bharosemand hona)
* **Strength** (taqat)
* **Durability** (lambi umar)
* **Stability** (stable rehna)
  Note: Robustness = all of these together in systems
  (robustness in sab ka combined idea hai)

---

## 9. 🚫 Antonyms

* Fragility (kamzori)
* Weakness (kamzor hona)
* Instability (unstable hona)
  (ulta matlab hota hai jab cheez easily break ho jaye)

---

## 10. 🧩 Word Family

* **Robust** (adjective) = strong
* **Robustly** (adverb) = strongly
* **Robustness** (noun) = strength/stability
  (ye sab same root word se bane hain)

---

## 11. 💬 Common Phrases

* Robust system
* Robust design
* Robust performance
* Robust algorithm
  (ye phrases CS aur engineering mein common hain)

---

## 12. 🧠 Memory Technique

Think: **ROBUST = Robot + Strong**
A robot that never breaks easily = robustness
(robustness ko robot jaisa strong imagine karo jo easily fail nahi hota)

---

## 13. ⚠️ Common Mistakes Students Make

* Thinking robustness = only “fast performance” ❌
* Confusing it with “accuracy” ❌
* Forgetting it includes handling errors ❌
  (robustness sirf fast hona nahi hota, balkay errors handle karna bhi hota hai)

---

# ✍️ SENTENCES

---

## 1. 🏠 Daily-Life Sentences

* This chair shows good robustness in daily use. (ye kursi roz ke use mein strong rehti hai)
* My phone has good robustness even after falling. (mera phone girne ke baad bhi theek chal raha hai)
* Robustness of this bag makes it very useful for travel. (ye bag safar ke liye bohat mazboot hai)

---

## 2. 🎓 Academic Sentences

* The robustness of a theory is tested under different conditions. (theory ko alag halaton mein check kiya jata hai)
* Researchers study robustness to improve system performance. (researchers system ko behtar banane ke liye robustness study karte hain)
* The experiment shows high robustness in results. (experiment ke results bohat stable hain)

---

## 3. 💻 Computer Science Sentences (ACTIVE VOICE)

* The programmer builds robustness into the software. (programmer software ko strong banata hai)
* The system handles errors to improve robustness. (system errors handle karta hai taake robustness improve ho)
* Developers test robustness using random inputs. (developers random inputs se system check karte hain)
* The algorithm increases robustness by managing failures. (algorithm failures handle karke system ko strong banata hai)
* Engineers design applications that improve robustness. (engineers applications ko zyada stable banate hain)

---

## 4. 💻 Computer Science Sentences (PASSIVE VOICE)

* Robustness is improved by handling unexpected errors. (unexpected errors handle karke robustness behtar hoti hai)
* The software is tested for robustness under stress conditions. (software ko tough conditions mein test kiya jata hai)
* Errors are managed to increase system robustness. (errors ko manage karke system strong banaya jata hai)
* Random inputs are used to check robustness. (random inputs use karke robustness test ki jati hai)
* System failures are reduced to improve robustness. (failures kam karke system ko strong banaya jata hai)
