## 📘 Word: **Heuristic**

---

## 1. 🧠 Basic Meaning

A **heuristic** is a **simple rule or shortcut** that helps you solve a problem quickly, but it may not be perfect.
(Heuristic aik simple rule ya shortcut hota hai jo masla jaldi solve karne mein help karta hai lekin 100% perfect nahi hota)

---

## 2. 🗣️ Roman Urdu Meaning

**Heuristic = aisa simple tareeqa jo fast solution deta hai, lekin guarantee nahi hoti ke best ho**
(Heuristic ka matlab aik quick method jo jaldi jawab deta hai magar best answer zaroori nahi hota)

---

## 3. ⭐ Why This Word Matters

In Computer Science, we often deal with **big problems**.
Exact solution sometimes takes too long, so we use heuristics to get a **good enough answer quickly**.
(Bari problems mein exact solution bohat time leta hai is liye heuristic use hota hai)

---

## 4. 🌍 Daily-Life Usage

We use heuristics in daily life without knowing it:

* Choosing shortest line at a shop
* Guessing fastest road in traffic
* Picking food you already like
  (Hum rozana shortcuts use karte hain faisla jaldi karne ke liye)

---

## 5. 🎓 Academic Usage

In studies, a heuristic helps students:

* Solve math problems faster
* Make quick guesses in exams
* Understand patterns easily
  (Studies mein heuristic students ko fast thinking mein help karta hai)

---

## 6. 💻 Computer Science Usage

In CS, heuristics are used in:

* Search algorithms
* AI decision making
* Problem-solving when exact answer is expensive
  (Computer science mein heuristic AI aur algorithms ko fast decisions lene mein help karta hai)

---

## 7. 👶 Beginner Explanation

Think of heuristic as a **shortcut rule**.
Instead of finding the perfect answer, you find a **good enough answer quickly**.
(Heuristic aik shortcut jaisa hota hai jo perfect nahi hota lekin kaam chal jata hai)

---

## 8. 🔁 Synonyms vs Similar Words

**Synonyms:**

* Rule of thumb
* Shortcut
* Approximation method

**Similar words:**

* Algorithm (more exact steps)
* Guess (less structured)
  (Algorithm strict hota hai, heuristic flexible hota hai)

---

## 9. ⛔ Antonyms

* Exact method
* Precise algorithm
* Deterministic solution
  (Heuristic ke opposite perfect aur exact methods hotay hain)

---

## 10. 🧬 Word Family

* Heuristic (noun/adjective)
* Heuristics (plural)
* Heuristically (adverb)
  (Ye word different forms mein use hota hai)

---

## 11. 💬 Common Phrases

* Heuristic approach
* Heuristic method
* Heuristic search
  (Ye phrases CS aur AI mein bohat common hain)

---

## 12. 🧠 Memory Technique

Think: **“Heuristic = Hurry + Trick”**
You are **hurrying** to find a **trick solution**
(Heuristic yaad rakhne ka simple tareeqa hai jaldi wala trick)

---

## 13. ⚠️ Common Mistakes Students Make

* Thinking heuristic means “wrong answer” (ye wrong nahi hota, bas approximate hota hai)
* Confusing heuristic with exact algorithm
* Using it only as “guessing” without structure
  (Heuristic sirf guess nahi hota, ye structured shortcut hota hai)

# ✍️ Sentences
