## 📘 Word: **brute**

---

## **1. Basic Meaning**

A **brute** means a rough, violent, or very cruel person or animal.
(Itna sakht ya be-reham insaan ya janwar jo dard deta ho)

---

## **2. Roman Urdu Meaning**

**Brute = sakht, be-reham ya jangli type insaan ya janwar**
(Brute ka matlab koi aisa banda ya cheez jo harsh ya violent ho)

---

## **3. Why This Word Matters**

This word helps you describe behavior that is not gentle or kind.
(Yeh word un logon ya cheezon ko describe karne ke liye use hota hai jo soft ya kind na hon)

---

## **4. Daily-Life Usage**

You can use it when someone behaves very roughly or aggressively.
(Jab koi banda bohat sakhti ya gusse se behave kare to yeh word use hota hai)

---

## **5. Academic Usage**

In writing, “brute” can describe harsh methods or uncontrolled actions.
(Academic writing mein brute ka use strong ya uncontrolled actions ke liye hota hai)

---

## **6. Computer Science Usage**

In CS, “brute force” means trying all possible solutions without smart thinking.
(Computer Science mein brute force ka matlab har possible tareeqa try karna hota hai)

---

## **7. Beginner Explanation**

Think of a brute as someone who solves problems by force, not intelligence.
(Beginner level pe brute wo hota hai jo dimagh se nahi balke force se kaam kare)

---

## **8. Synonyms vs Similar Words**

* Synonyms: **cruel, savage, rough**
* Similar: **aggressive, harsh**
  (Synonyms wo words hain jo same meaning ke qareeb hotay hain)

---

## **9. Antonyms**

* gentle
* kind
* soft
  (Antonyms wo words hotay hain jo ulta meaning dete hain)

---

## **10. Word Family**

* brute (noun)
* brutal (adjective)
* brutality (noun)
  (Word family matlab related words jo same root se aate hain)

---

## **11. Common Phrases**

* brute force
* brute strength
* brute attack
  (Ye common expressions hain jahan brute use hota hai)

---

## **12. Memory Technique**

Think: “Brute = strong like a wild animal.”
(Yad rakhne ka tareeqa: brute ko jungle ke ziddi janwar se compare karo)

---

## **13. Common Mistakes Students Make**

Students think “brute” always means animal, but it can also describe methods like brute force.
(Student aksar sochte hain brute sirf janwar hota hai, lekin ye methods ke liye bhi use hota hai)

---

# ✍️ SENTENCES

---

## **1. Daily-Life Sentences**

* The man acted like a brute in the fight. (Ladai mein woh banda bohat zyada sakht aur aggressive behave kar raha tha)
* Don’t be a brute with your younger brother. (Apne chotay bhai ke saath itni sakhti mat karo)
* The brute scared everyone in the street. (Woh sakht aur dara dene wala insaan sab ko road par dara raha tha)

---

## **2. Academic Sentences**

* The brute behavior of the character shows lack of control. (Character ka harsh behavior control ki kami ko show karta hai)
* A brute approach is not suitable for problem-solving in studies. (Sirf force wala tareeqa parhai ke masail ke liye theek nahi hota)
* The study explains brute actions in social environments. (Study social environment mein harsh actions ko explain karti hai)

---

## **3. Computer Science Sentences (ACTIVE VOICE)**

* The programmer uses a brute force method to solve the puzzle. (Programmer puzzle solve karne ke liye brute force method use karta hai)
* The algorithm checks all possibilities in a brute force way. (Algorithm har possible option brute force tareeqe se check karta hai)
* The system applies brute force to crack the simple password. (System simple password ko break karne ke liye brute force apply karta hai)
* Developers test brute force techniques on small datasets. (Developers chotay data sets par brute force techniques test karte hain)
* The student writes a program that uses brute force searching. (Student ek program likhta hai jo brute force searching use karta hai)

---

## **4. Computer Science Sentences (PASSIVE VOICE)**

* A brute force method is used by the programmer to solve the puzzle. (Puzzle solve karne ke liye programmer ke zariye brute force method use kiya jata hai)
* All possibilities are checked by the algorithm in a brute force approach. (Algorithm ke zariye har possibility brute force tareeqe se check ki jati hai)
* A simple password is cracked by the system using brute force. (Simple password system ke zariye brute force se crack kiya jata hai)
* Brute force techniques are tested on small datasets by developers. (Developers ke zariye chotay datasets par brute force techniques test ki jati hain)
* A searching program is written using brute force by the student. (Student ke zariye brute force use karke searching program likha jata hai)
