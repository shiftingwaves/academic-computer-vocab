# 📘 Word: **derivation**

## 1. Basic Meaning

**Derivation** means getting something from another source or origin.
(Kisi cheez ko doosri cheez se hasil karna)

It can also mean:

* finding an answer step-by-step in math
* showing how a word or formula was formed

---

## 2. Roman Urdu Meaning

**Derivation** =

* “Nikalna”
* “Kisi source se hasil karna”
* “Step-by-step result banana”
  (Yani kisi cheez ko kisi aur cheez se samajh kar nikalna)

---

## 3. Why This Word Matters

This word is important in:

* mathematics
* programming
* linguistics
* algorithms
* science subjects

Computer Science students use it in:

* formulas
* algorithms
* logic
* data processing

(CS mein formulas aur logical steps samajhne ke liye yeh lafz bohat common hai)

---

## 4. Daily-Life Usage

People use **derivation** when talking about:

* where something came from
* how an idea was created
* the origin of words

Example:
“The derivation of this word is interesting.”
(Is lafz ki origin interesting hai)

---

## 5. Academic Usage

In academics, **derivation** means:

* solving formulas step-by-step
* proving equations
* explaining origins

Example:
“The teacher explained the derivation of the formula.”
(Teacher ne formula step-by-step samjhaya)

---

## 6. Computer Science Usage

In Computer Science, **derivation** is used in:

* algorithms
* formal languages
* automata
* compiler design
* mathematical proofs

Example:
A parser uses derivation rules in grammar.
(Parser grammar ke rules follow karta hai)

---

## 7. Beginner Explanation

Think of **derivation** like this:

👉 “How did we get this answer?”
(Yeh answer kis tarah nikala gaya)

Or:

👉 “Where did this thing come from?”
(Yeh cheez kis source se ayi)

---

## 13. Common Mistakes Students Make

❌ Thinking derivation only belongs to math
✔ It is also used in language and Computer Science.

❌ Confusing “derivation” with “definition”
✔ Derivation = how something comes
✔ Definition = meaning of something

❌ Skipping steps in derivation
✔ Derivation should show clear steps.
