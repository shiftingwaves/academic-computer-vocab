## 📘 Word: **vice**

---

## 1. 🎯 Basic Meaning

**Vice** means a bad habit or immoral behavior that is not good for a person or society.
(Vice ka matlab hai koi buri aadat ya ghalat/na-akhlaqi rawayya)

---

## 2. 🟢 Roman Urdu Meaning

**Vice = buri aadat / na-akhlaqi amal / ghalat rawayya**
(Vice woh cheez hoti hai jo insaan ke character ko bigar deti hai)

---

## 3. ⭐ Why This Word Matters

This word is important because it helps us understand **good vs bad behavior** in life and society.
(Yeh word samajhna important hai kyun ke yeh humein sahi aur ghalat mein farq batata hai)

---

## 4. 🏠 Daily-Life Usage

We use “vice” when talking about bad habits like lying, smoking, or wasting time.
(Daily life mein vice buri aadaton ke liye use hota hai)

---

## 5. 🎓 Academic Usage

In essays and moral studies, students discuss vice as behavior that harms individuals or society.
(Academic writing mein vice ko society ke liye nuqsan-deh amal ke taur par discuss kiya jata hai)

---

## 6. 💻 Computer Science Usage

In computer science, “vice” is not a technical term, but it can describe **bad coding habits** like poor design or unsafe practices.
(CS mein vice formal term nahi hai, lekin buri coding habits ke liye use ho sakta hai)

---

## 7. 🧠 Beginner Explanation

Think of vice as:
👉 “Something I should not do because it is bad for me.”
(Vice ka simple matlab: woh kaam jo mujhe nahi karna chahiye kyun ke woh ghalat hai)

---

## 8. 🔁 Synonyms vs Similar Words

**Synonyms:**

* bad habit
* immorality
* wrongdoing

**Similar Words:**

* addiction
* fault
* weakness

---

## 9. ❌ Antonyms

* virtue
* goodness
* morality
* discipline

---

## 10. 📚 Word Family

* vice (noun)
* vicious (adjective)
* viciously (adverb)

---

## 11. 💬 Common Phrases

* a vice of society
* a harmful vice
* free from vice
* overcome a vice

---

## 12. 🧠 Memory Technique

Think: **VICE = “Very Immoral Choice Everytime”**
(Yaad rakho ke vice hamesha ghalat choice hoti hai)

---

## 13. ⚠️ Common Mistakes Students Make

* Confusing **vice (bad habit)** with “vise” (a tool for holding things)
* Using it for normal mistakes instead of serious bad habits
* Thinking it only means crime (it can also mean small bad habits)

# ✏️ Sentences

---

## 🏠 1. Daily-Life Sentences (3)

* Smoking is a harmful vice. (Smoking ek buri aadat hai)
* He is trying to remove his vice of wasting time. (Woh apni time waste karne ki aadat khatam kar raha hai)
* Lying is a bad vice in society. (Jhoot bolna society mein buri aadat hai)

---

## 🎓 2. Academic Sentences (3)

* The essay discusses the vice of corruption in society. (Essay mein corruption ki buri aadat discuss ki gayi hai)
* Students should avoid the vice of plagiarism. (Students ko copy-paste ki buri aadat se bachna chahiye)
* The research highlights the vice of inequality. (Research ne na-insafi ki buri aadat ko highlight kiya hai)

---

## 💻 3. Computer Science Sentences (Active Voice) (5)

* The developer avoids the vice of writing unsafe code. (Developer unsafe code likhne ki buri aadat se bachta hai)
* The programmer removes the vice of poor coding style. (Programmer weak coding style ki buri aadat door karta hai)
* The team identifies the vice in the software design. (Team software design ki problem wali aadat ko identify karti hai)
* The engineer fixes the vice of inefficient algorithms. (Engineer inefficient algorithms ki buri practice ko theek karta hai)
* The student learns to avoid the vice of hardcoding values. (Student hardcoding values ki buri aadat se bachna seekhta hai)

---

## 💻 4. Computer Science Sentences (Passive Voice) (5)

* The vice of poor design is avoided by good developers. (Achay developers poor design ki buri aadat se bachte hain)
* Unsafe coding practices are considered a vice in programming. (Unsafe coding practices programming mein buri aadat samjhi jati hain)
* The vice of duplication is removed by refactoring. (Duplication ki buri aadat refactoring se khatam hoti hai)
* Bad habits in code are identified by code review as a vice. (Code review buri coding habits ko vice ke taur par detect karta hai)
* Inefficient logic is treated as a vice in software development. (Inefficient logic software development mein buri aadat samjhi jati hai)
